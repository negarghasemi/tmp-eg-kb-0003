# EnerGuide for Products

Through the EnerGuide labeling system, consumers can find the most efficient products in their class, ranging from major household appliances to heating and cooling products.

## Key EnerGuide Information

### The EnerGuide Label
*Know the energy a product uses and how it compares to other models before you purchase.*

### Labelling Information for Manufacturers and Importers
*Instructions on how to properly label prescribed products and regulatory requirements.*

### Energy Efficient Information for Retailers and Dealers
*Retailers or dealers of energy-using products can help Canadians make energy-efficient choices.*

### Energy Efficiency Regulations
*The Regulations stipulate whether the label is mandatory, how it should be displayed, and what elements it must contain.*

### Welcome to My EnerGuide for Homes
*A nationally recognized rating tool for energy efficiency programs across Canada.*
