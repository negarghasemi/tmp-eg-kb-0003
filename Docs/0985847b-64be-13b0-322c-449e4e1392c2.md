# Oil-fired furnaces

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory definition

**Oil-fired furnace**: a warm-air furnace, other than a furnace for a mobile home or recreational vehicle that has an input rate of not more than 65.92 kilowatts (225 000 Btu/h) and that is either exclusively oil-fired or capable of being fired, at the choice of the user, by either oil or another fuel.

### Compliance date

**Manufactured on or after**: July 3, 2019

_For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations_

### Compliant product models

*   [Oil-fired furnaces](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=Furnaces.Oil) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Testing standard

*   **CSA P.2**: for annual fuel utilization efficiency
*   **10 C.F.R. Appendix AA**: for FER

### Energy efficiency standard

**Energy performance standard for oil-fired furnaces**

| Product class                                          | Minimum annual fuel utilization efficiency | FER                                                              |
| :----------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  66 **kW** (225 000 **Btu/h**)                        |  83%                                      |  0.071  Q<sub>Max</sub> + 382                                  |
|                                                        |                                            | FER  FER for product class Non-Weatherized, Non-Condensing Oil Furnace Fan (NWO-NC), set out in 10 C.F.R. 430.32(y) |

*   **FER**: fan energy rating
*   **kW**: kilowatts
*   **Btu/h**: British thermal units per hour
*   **Q<sub>Max</sub>**: airflow in cfm

### Energy efficiency report requirements

Manufacturers must submit an energy efficiency report including the following:

*   name of product
*   brand name
*   model number
*   manufacturer
*   name of the certification body or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
*   whether a mathematical model as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
*   input rate in kW (Btu/h)
*   annual fuel utilization efficiency
*   FER, expressed in W/472 L/s (W/1,000 ft/min)
*   maximum airflow (Q<sub>max</sub>), expressed in L/s (ft/min)

_Sign in to the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/) (CREED) to download and submit an energy efficiency report for this product._
