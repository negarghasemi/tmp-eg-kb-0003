# FAQs Electricity Rates

## General Information on Electricity Rates

### How are electricity prices set?
Electricity prices are determined by the **Regulated Price Plan (RPP)**. This plan forecasts regulated prices for:
*   **Ontario Power Generation's (OPG)** nuclear and base load hydro facilities (operating 24/7).
*   Existing contract prices for supply from non-utility generators.
*   The **Ontario Energy Board's (OEB)** forecast of electricity prices in the open electricity market over the next 12 months.

The OEB has also expanded the ratio between on-peak and off-peak prices from **2:1 to 2.1:1**. *This change aims to encourage customers to shift their electricity usage to off-peak periods (evenings and weekends) for greater savings.*

### Why does the Ontario Energy Board adjust electricity prices every 12 months?
The OEB reviews and may readjust prices annually to ensure they accurately reflect the cost paid to generators. The **Regulated Price Plan** helps to smooth these prices for consumers, allowing them to pay the true cost of electricity over time without being subject to daily market volatility.

### What can I do if I can't pay my bill (fixed income)?
Several options are available for financial assistance:
*   **Ontario Electricity Support Program (OESP)**: Provides a monthly on-bill credit to eligible low-income consumers. The credit amount varies based on the number of residents in the home and combined household income. *Visit the Ontario Electricity Support website for more information.*
*   **Low Income Energy Assistance Plan (LEAP)**: Offers emergency financial assistance. *Contact the Oak Park Neighbourhood Centre or Michelle Knoll at 905-257-6029 for details.*
*   **Equal Payment Plan (EPP)**: Allows for a fixed monthly withdrawal from your bank account, calculated based on your average consumption over the previous 12 months. This amount is reviewed annually and may be adjusted.
*   **Direct Contact**: You can contact Oakville Hydro at **905-825-9400 extension 4463** to discuss and arrange payment terms.

### Is there anything I can do to decrease my bill?
**Oakville Hydro** provides tools and initiatives to help customers manage energy usage and costs:
*   **MyOakvilleHydro online portal**: Allows customers to:
    *   View account details.
    *   See and manage electricity usage.
    *   Review personal time-of-use dashboard.
    *   Access mobile features, eBilling (paperless bills), enhanced time-of-use charts, and data downloads.
    *   Utilize a price plan comparison tool.
    *   Receive alerts for predicted time-of-use charges.
    *   Use the \"Pay My Bill\" option.
*   **Conservation and Demand Management initiatives**: Oakville Hydro is committed to promoting energy conservation and offers opportunities for customers to actively participate.

### Does Oakville Hydro keep all of the money it collects?
**No**, Oakville Hydro acts as a billing agent, collecting money on behalf of other electricity market participants and the government. **Oakville Hydro's portion of a residential customers electricity bill is approximately 20%**, which is included in the **delivery charge** line item on electricity bills.

### What is the role of a local distribution company, such as Oakville Hydro?
*   **Electricity Transmission**: High-voltage transmission lines transport electricity from generating stations to local distribution areas. **Hydro One** owns and operates most of these lines in Ontario.
*   **Electricity Delivery**: **Oakville Hydro** is responsible for delivering electricity through its wires directly to homes and businesses within its service area.
