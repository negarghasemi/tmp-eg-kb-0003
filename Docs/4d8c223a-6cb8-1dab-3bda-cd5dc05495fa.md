# Refrigerators and Refrigerator-Freezers

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition
*   **Combination refrigerator-freezer**: A household combination refrigerator-freezer with a defrost system (including compressor-cycled automatic defrost) and a capacity of 1105 L (39 cubic feet) or less.
*   **Refrigerator**: A household refrigerator with a capacity of 1105 L (39 cubic feet) or less and a defrost system (including compressor-cycled automatic defrost). Excludes household refrigerators using an absorption refrigeration system or miscellaneous refrigeration products.

### Compliance Date
*   **Manufactured on or after**: September 15, 2014
*   *For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations.*

### Compliant Product Models
*   [Refrigerators and refrigerator-freezers as listed on NRCans searchable database](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=Refr_Refr-freezers)
