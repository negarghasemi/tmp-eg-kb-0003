# Savings examples

## Choose ENERGY STAR when you are bulk buying

![Energy Star logo](/sites/nrcan/files/energy/images/energystar/logo_high-efficiency_e.gif)

Your organization can save money and reduce its environmental impact by choosing ENERGY STAR certified products.

### How to get started:
-   Find out about the [benefits and features](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products) of each ENERGY STAR certified product.
-   Use the [searchable list of products](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/) to find certified models of over 75 product types available in Canada.
-   Access the [ENERGY STAR Simple Savings Calculator](http://oee.nrcan.gc.ca/residential/business/energystar/procurement/calculator.cfm) to compare the economic and environmental benefits of up to three models of a single type of product.
-   **Get a snapshot of savings** using the Simple Savings Calculator, which includes calculated energy use, operating costs and related environmental impacts for these common products:
    -   [Computers](#a1a)
    -   [Griddles](#a1b)
    -   [Lighting products](#a1c)
    -   [Refrigerators](#a1d)
    -   [Televisions](#a1e)
    -   [Ventilating fans](#a1f)
    -   [Water heaters  tankless](#a1g)

*Access the [ENERGY STAR Simple Savings Calculator](http://oee.nrcan.gc.ca/residential/business/energystar/procurement/calculator.cfm) and localize the settings by selecting a province or territory, or inputting your own data.*

*Consult the [ENERGY STAR product specifications](https://www.energystar.gov/products/spec) for technical information on certified products.*

*More information about how [ENERGY STAR procurement](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/procurement) can help your organizations bottom line.*
