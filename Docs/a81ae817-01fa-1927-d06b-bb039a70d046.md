# Compliance and Enforcement: Energy-using products

![Energy Efficiency Products](/sites/nrcan/files/oee/images/energystar/images/EnergyEfficiency_Products.png)

Natural Resources Canada is responsible for compliance monitoring and enforcement activities to ensure that the requirements of the **Energy Efficiency Act** and **Regulations** are met. These requirements apply to dealers of regulated energy-using products who import products, or ship between provinces or territories, for the purpose of sale or lease.

Within NRCan, the **Office of Energy Efficiencys Compliance Operations group** undertakes compliance promotion and monitoring activities such as dealer education and outreach, report verification, product testing, and market surveillance.

The [Compliance and Enforcement Policy (2020)](/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/compliance-enforcement-policy-energy-efficiency-act-energy-efficiency-regulations-2020) outlines the compliance and enforcement activities conducted in relation to energy-using products that fall under the *Energy Efficiency Act*.
