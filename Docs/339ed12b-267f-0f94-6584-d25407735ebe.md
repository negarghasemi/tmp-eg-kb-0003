# Technical Standards Documents - Natural Resources Canada

## Overview

The *Energy Efficiency Act* grants authority to incorporate technical standards documents (TSDs) by reference into the *Energy Efficiency Regulations*. This is done to harmonize with other jurisdictions.

A TSD is a document published in both official languages by the Minister of Energy and Natural Resources. It adapts, combines, or reproduces, in whole or in part, documents from jurisdictions, standards development organizations, or industry associations. These documents set out requirements or guidance for energy-using products or classes of energy-using products, with adaptations for the Canadian context.

For more information on the regulatory process for TSDs, visit the [Canada's Energy Efficiency Regulations - Regulatory process webpage](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/canada-s-energy-efficiency-regulations). For specific questions, contact [EEregulations-reglementEE@nrcan-rncan.gc.ca](mailto:EEregulations-reglementEE@nrcan-rncan.gc.ca).
