# Oil to Heat Pump Affordability (OHPA) Program at a Glance

*As of February 2026*

## Canada-Wide Statistics

- **New Heat Pumps Installed**: 29,798
- **Applications**: 54,007
- **Bonus Cheques Issued**: 29,651
- **Average Grant**: $11,451
- **Grants Issued**: 31,379
- **Total Issued to Date**: $359.3M

## Highlights

- **Greenhouse Gas Reductions**: **82,838 metric tonnes**
    - *Equivalent to 25,418 cars off the road*
- **Average Annual Household Savings (OHPA participants)**:
    - **Energy Costs**: $1,377
    - **Greenhouse Gas Reductions**: 2.78 tonnes
