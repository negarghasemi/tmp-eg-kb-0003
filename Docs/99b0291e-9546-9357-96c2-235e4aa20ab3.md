# Energy Efficiency Regulations

_This document provides up-to-date information on energy-using products regulated in Canada and on amendments to Canadas Energy Efficiency Regulations._

-   **[Amendment 18](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/amendment-18)** _Published in the [Canada Gazette, Part II](https://gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2025/2025-04-09/html/sor-dors110-eng.html)_
-   **[Technical standards documents](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/technical-standards-documents)**

## Topics

-   **[Announcements](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/regulatory-announcements)**: Amendments to the Regulations, their stage of development and public consultation opportunities, and information on the Energy Efficiency Act.
-   **[Planning and reporting](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/planning-reporting)**: An overview of future amendments through the Forward Regulatory Plan and reporting requirements.
-   **[Regulatory process](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/canada-s-energy-efficiency-regulations)**: Information on the regulatory process, including Ministerial Regulations and Technical Standards Documents.
-   **[Guide to the Regulations](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/guide-canada-s-energy-efficiency-regulations)**: How the Regulations work, who is affected by them and what are the responsibilities to comply with them.
-   **[Technical standards documents](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/technical-standards-documents)**: Technical standard documents incorporated by reference in the Energy Efficiency Regulations.
-   **[Searchable product list](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/)**: Find and compare the energy efficiency of all product models regulated in Canada.
-   **[Importers Corner](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/importers-corner)**: Information and instructions for dealers who are importing regulated products into Canada.
-   **[Online reporting system](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/compliance-regulatory-energy-efficiency-database-creed)**: The new Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED) is the online system dealers will use to submit compliance and energy efficiency information.
-   **[Compliance and Enforcement](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/compliance-enforcement-energy-products)**: Information for dealers of regulated products on compliance to the Energy Efficiency Regulations.
-   **[Regulatory co-operation](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/regulatory-co-operation)**: Canada-U.S. and federal-provincial-territorial efforts to better align energy efficiency standards.
-   **[Market transformation](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/market-transformation)**: Governments plans to accelerate the adoption of high efficiency technologies in support of the transition to a low-carbon building sector.
