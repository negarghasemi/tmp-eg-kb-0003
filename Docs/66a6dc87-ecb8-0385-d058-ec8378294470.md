# External Power Supplies (EPS)

## Overview

**Looking for an energy-efficient external power supply (EPS)?**

An EPS is a separate component that converts AC power to a lower AC or DC voltage for electronic devices such as printers and game consoles.

## Energy Efficiency Requirements

*   In Canada, EPS must meet **minimum performance standards** set by Canadas Energy Efficiency Regulations.
*   EPS units must meet **international energy consumption requirements**.
*   Most external power supplies are subject to Canadas *Energy Efficiency Regulations*, which establish energy efficiency standards to eliminate the least efficient products from the Canadian market.

## International Efficiency Marking Protocol

*   The **ENERGY STAR initiative in Canada does not support External Power Supply (EPS) or Adaptors**.
*   For **ENERGY STAR certified products** that require an EPS, the EPS must meet **level VI or higher performance requirements** under the International Efficiency Marking Protocol.
*   These EPS units must include the **level VI Roman numeral marking**.
*   The U.S. Department of Energy, which administers this protocol, increased the stringency of these requirements in Version 3, published September 2013.
