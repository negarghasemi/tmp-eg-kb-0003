# Bundle upgrades and save

A home energy assessment will help you identify the best energy efficiency solutions for your home. Bundle two or more upgrades for bigger rebates, energy savings* and comfort. Bundle three and get an extra $500.

## Rebates

Start with an initial home energy assessment, then complete at least two energy-saving* upgrades to qualify for rebates.

### Insulation Rebates

-   **Attic insulation**: Up to **$1,500**
-   **Foundation insulation**: Up to **$2,300**
-   **Exposed floor insulation**: **$300**
-   **Exterior wall insulation**: Up to **$3,600**

### Other Rebates

-   **Windows and doors**: **$100 per rough opening**
-   **Heat pump water heaters**: **$500**
-   **Air sealing**: Up to **$250**
    _To qualify, your home needs to meet a specific air tightness target. Since results can vary, we recommend choosing air sealing as your third upgrade or later to help maintain your eligibility._

### Bundle Bonus

**Bundle three upgrades, get $500!**
_Thinking of upgrading more? Complete three qualifying upgrades for an additional $500 rebate._

### Home Energy Assessments

-   **Rebate**: **$600**
    _For these upgrades, an initial home energy assessment is required before beginning any work. A registered energy advisor will assess your home and uncover energy-saving opportunities. To offset the cost of the initial and follow-up home energy assessments, you will receive a $600 rebate after successful completion of the program. Where an owner has tenants, the owner must apply._

## How to get your rebates

1.  **Schedule an assessment**
    Before beginning any work, fill out the form below to get a list of Service Organizations in your area. Contact a Service Organization and complete an initial home energy assessment.
2.  **Get a custom report**
    See which upgrades are recommended for your home and choose at least two to complete.
3.  **Complete 2+ upgrades**
    Have your preferred contractor complete the work.
4.  **Schedule a follow-up assessment**
    After upgrades are installed, complete a final assessment with the same registered energy advisor.
5.  **Receive your rebates**
    After your follow-up home energy assessment is completed and your registered energy advisor submits all required documents, your cheque should arrive by mail within 60 days.

## Is your home eligible?

-   To qualify for the **Home Renovation Savings program**, you must own your home.
-   Eligible housing types include single detached or semi-detached homes, row/townhomes and mobile homes on a permanent foundation.
-   Be an Enbridge Gas customer with an active account and primarily heat your home with a natural gas furnace or boiler OR be connected to the Ontario electricity grid and heat your home with electricity, propane, oil or wood.

_ Cornwall Electric customers who heat their homes with electricity, propane, oil, or wood are connected to the Hydro-Qubec electricity grid and are not eligible to participate unless they are converting to or from Enbridge natural gas for home heating._

                                                                                                     # Home Renovation Savings Program: Energy Assessment Required Upgrades

## Program Overview

An initial home energy assessment helps identify the best energy efficiency solutions for your home. By bundling two or more upgrades, you can qualify for bigger rebates, energy savings, and enhanced comfort. Complete three upgrades and receive an extra **$500 bonus**.

## Rebates Available with Energy Assessment

To qualify for these rebates, you must start with an initial home energy assessment, then complete at least two energy-saving upgrades.

### Home Energy Assessment Rebate

-   **$600** reimbursement to offset the cost of initial and follow-up assessments upon successful program completion.

## How to Get Your Rebates (Steps)

1.  **Schedule an assessment**: Before any work, complete an initial home energy assessment with a registered energy advisor from an approved service organization. (Fill out the form on the website for a list).
2.  **Get a custom report**: Receive a report with recommended upgrades and choose at least two to complete.
3.  **Complete 2+ upgrades**: Have your preferred contractor install the chosen upgrades.
4.  **Schedule a follow-up assessment**: After upgrades are installed, complete a final assessment with the same registered energy advisor.
5.  **Receive your rebates**: Your rebates will be sent after your energy advisor submits all required documents.

## Other Rebates (No Home Energy Assessment Required)

You may also be eligible for rebates for the following measures, which do not require a home energy assessment:

-   Heat pumps
-   Solar panels plus battery storage
-   Smart thermostats

## Eligibility Requirements

-   **Homeownership**: You must own your home.
-   **Housing Types**: Eligible types include single detached or semi-detached homes, row/townhomes, and mobile homes on a permanent foundation. _Stacked townhomes are not eligible._
-   **Heating Source**: You must be an Enbridge Gas customer primarily heating your home with natural gas OR be connected to the Ontario electricity grid and heat your home with electricity, propane, oil, or wood.
    -   _Cornwall Electric customers heating with electricity, propane, or oil are generally not eligible unless converting to or from Enbridge natural gas for home heating._
