# Energy Affordability Program

The Energy Affordability Program offers **no-cost energy-saving upgrades** tailored to the specific needs of your home. This program aims to help income-eligible electricity consumers better manage their monthly electricity costs and increase home comfort. All expert advice and energy-saving home upgrades are completely free of charge.

## Program Overview

The program provides two main types of support:

*   **Comprehensive Support**: In-home energy needs assessment, upgrades, and appliance replacements.
*   **Energy Saving Kits**: Kits with easy-to-install energy-saving products.

### Special Funding for Cold Climate Air Source Heat Pumps

Thanks to funding from Natural Resources Canada (NRCan) through its Oil to Heat Pump Affordability (OHPA) program, eligible homeowners with oil-fueled heating systems may now qualify to receive a **high-efficiency cold climate heat pump system at no cost**. These systems can help reduce energy costs, improve home comfort, increase property value, and lower carbon footprint.

## Comprehensive Support

Ontario residents who qualify for this level of support will receive a visit from an energy-efficiency expert to conduct a **free energy needs assessment**. This assessment identifies opportunities to save energy, and participants may receive upgrades and replace eligible appliances.

### What's Included (Comprehensive Support)

*   **LED light bulbs**
*   **Energy-efficient appliances**:
    *   Refrigerators
    *   Freezers
    *   Dehumidifiers
    *   Window air conditioner
*   **Other energy-saving items**:
    *   Drying rack for clothes
    *   Smart power strip
    *   High-efficiency showerheads (*standard and handheld, for homes with electric hot water heating*)
    *   Faucet aerators (*kitchen and bathroom, for homes with electric hot water heating*)
*   **Home envelope improvements** (*for homes heated by electricity or oil*):
    *   Additional attic or basement insulation
    *   Draft proofing
    *   Programmable or smart thermostat(s)
    *   Cold climate air source heat pump

### Eligibility (Comprehensive Support)

To qualify, participants must be:

1.  A resident of an eligible Social Housing property, **OR**
2.  An individual who owns, rents, or leases a residence, is listed as the primary or secondary utility account holder, **AND** meets **ONE** of the following criteria:
    *   Has an annual household income for the previous year that does not exceed the following limits:

| **6**                        | **$118,113**                |\

    *   Received assistance from one of the following programs in the past 12 months:
        *   Allowance for Seniors
        *   Allowance for the Survivor

        *   Healthy Smiles Ontario Child Dental Program

## Energy Saving Kits

Eligible households can receive a **free energy saving kit** with easy-to-install products to help manage energy costs and improve home comfort. These kits include simple, self-installable products to seal drafts, save on lighting, and reduce water use.

### What's Included (Energy Saving Kits)

Depending on eligibility and existing home equipment, each kit may include:

*   **LED light bulbs**
*   **Weather-stripping**
*   **High-efficiency handheld showerhead**
*   **Faucet aerators** (*kitchen and bathroom*)
*   **Retractable clothesline**
*   **LED night light**
*   **Block heater timer**

_After applying, you'll receive your energy saving kit within three to six weeks by mail. There are no fees or shipping costs._

### Eligibility (Energy Saving Kits)

To qualify, participants must:

1.  Be an individual who owns, rents, or leases a residence in Ontario and is listed as the primary or secondary utility account holder.
2.  Have an annual household income for the previous year that does not exceed the following limits:

| Number of people in the home | Before-tax household income |
| :--------------------------- | :-------------------------- |\
| **1**                        | **$71,437**                 |\
| **2**                        | **$101,027**                |\
| **3**                        | **$123,731**                |\
| **4**                        | **$142,873**                |\
| **5**                        | **$159,737**                |\
| **6+**                       | **$174,983**                |
