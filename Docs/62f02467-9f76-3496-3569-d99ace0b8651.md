# Residential Electric Cooking Products

ENERGY STAR certified electric cooking products are **up to 10% more efficient** than standard models. They also contribute to healthier indoor air quality by not releasing pollutants associated with gas burning.

## Eligible Products

-   **Ranges (stoves)**
-   **Standalone/portable cooktops**

*Standalone ovens are not included in the certification.*

## Benefits of Electric Kitchen Appliances

Electric cooking appliances do not burn gas, which means they do not release hazardous gases such as:

-   Carbon monoxide
-   Nitrogen oxides
-   Formaldehyde
-   Other particulate matter

*Exposure to these pollutants has been linked to developing health disorders and adverse environmental effects.*

## Advantages of Induction Cooking

Induction cooktops and ranges use electromagnetic energy to heat cookware internally, offering several benefits:

-   **More efficient heating process** than traditional methods.
-   **Quicker heat-up times**.
-   **Better precision temperature control** compared to gas, radiant, or coil cooking.
-   The cooktop surface does **not get hot to the touch** without a pot or pan in place.

*Note: Induction cooktops require cookware made of ferromagnetic material (e.g., cast-iron, steel) or with a magnetic layer on the bottom.*

## Why ENERGY STAR Certified Electric Cooking Products Are Right for You

ENERGY STAR certified electric cooking products come in various styles, sizes, and functionalities to suit different needs:

### Functionality Options

-   **Slide-in range**: Sits flush between existing countertops.
-   **Freestanding range**: Can occupy a separate space.
-   **Cooktop**: Fits into your countertop.
-   **Combination sets**: Some products are sold as matching cooktop and oven sets, allowing separation of the cooktop from the oven.

### Range Width and Cooking Zones

-   Most cooking tops are **between 30 to 36 inches in width**, with other sizes available.
-   **Concentric cooking zones** accommodate different-sized cookware.
-   Some include **bridging elements** to combine heating zones for larger cookware (e.g., griddles).
-   **Specialty zones** for simmering or power heating are also offered on some models.

### Additional Product Features

-   **Smart features**: Allow connection to mobile apps or other devices.
-   **Cookware sensors**: Generate heat based on cookware size and location.
-   **Control locks**.
-   **Indicator lights** for active heating and hot surfaces.

### Capacity

-   Ovens included in electric ranges typically have a capacity of **5-6 cubic feet**.

## The ENERGY STAR Promise

All ENERGY STAR certified products are:

-   **Tested to meet strict efficiency specifications**.
-   **Certified by an independent third party**.
-   **Perform the same as or better than standard products** without compromising performance.

## Tip for Choosing an Energy Efficient Electric Cooking Product

-   When comparing similar models, consult the **EnerGuide label** to see the annual and lifetime energy consumption. **The lower the number, the better!**

## Regulations

Electric ranges are subject to **Canada's Energy Efficiency Regulations**, which establish minimum performance standards for energy efficiency. More details can be found in the [Guide to the Regulations](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/guide-canada-s-energy-efficiency-regulations).
