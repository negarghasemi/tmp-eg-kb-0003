# Energy Efficiency Reporting (EER) Templates

![Kitchen with a fridge; a man charging an electric car; a wall thermostat; a clothes washer and dryer](/sites/nrcan/files/oee/images/energystar/images/EnergyEfficiency_Products.png)

To support the submission of regulatory information, **Energy Efficient Reporting (EER) templates** for the following products and equipment are now available in collaboration with Open Government.

**Dealers** are required to use the product-specific Energy Efficiency Report (EER) templates to report regulated energy-using products to **Natural Resources Canada (NRCan)** prior to their first import. Completed EER templates must be submitted electronically to NRCan through the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/).

Please refer to the appropriate sections to access and download the most up-to-date templates for your reporting needs.
