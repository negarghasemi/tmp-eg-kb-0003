# Single package vertical air conditioners

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition
**Single package vertical air-conditioner**, a single package commercial air conditioner that is air-cooled and encased, with or without heating capability but not a heat pump.

### Compliance Date
*   Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2011**
*   *For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations*

### Compliant Product Models
*   [Single package vertical air conditioners](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=AC.SinglePackageVertical) as listed on NRCans searchable database

### Testing Standard
*   **CSA C746-17**

### Energy Efficiency Standard
**Energy performance Levels standard for single package vertical air conditioners (SPVAC)**

| Product type | Size                                                | Minimum efficiency requirement |
| :----------- | :-------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------- |
| SPVAC        | <19 kW (65,000 Btu/h)                               | EER =  11.0                   |
|              |  19 kW (65,000 Btu/h) and < 39.5 kW (135,000 Btu/h) | EER =  10.0                   |
|              |  39.5 kW (135,000 Btu/h) and < 70 kW (240,000 Btu/h) | EER =  10.0                   |

*   **COP** = Coefficient of performance
*   **EER** = Energy efficiency ratio
*   **Btu/h** = British thermal units per hour
*   **kW** = kilowatts

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements
To sign in to the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/) (CREED) to download and submit an energy efficiency report for this product, the following information is required:

*   **Name of product**
*   **Brand name**
*   **Model number**
*   **Manufacturer**
*   **Name of the certification body** or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
*   Whether a **mathematical model** as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
*   **AHRI classification**
*   **Cooling capacity** in kW (Btu/h)
*   **Energy efficiency ratio (EER)**
