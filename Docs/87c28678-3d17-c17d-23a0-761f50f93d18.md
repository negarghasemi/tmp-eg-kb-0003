# Shuba the Siberian: A Pet's View on Energy Efficiency

This report outlines practical energy-saving tips for homeowners, focusing on appliance maintenance and lighting upgrades.

## Energy-Saving Recommendations

### Dryer Maintenance

*   **Recommendation:** Regularly clean your dryer's lint trap.
    *   **Benefits:**
        *   Ensures **home safety** by preventing overheating.
        *   Improves dryer **efficiency**.
        *   Helps **save on energy** all year round.
*   **Go Further:** If you live in a condo, check for a **second lint trap** in your dryer's exhaust system and clean it regularly.

### Energy-Efficient Lighting

*   **Recommendation:** Switch to **ENERGY STAR certified LEDs** for your home lighting.
    *   **Benefits:**
        *   Use **70 to 90% less energy** than traditional incandescent bulbs.
        *   Can last up to **3.5 times longer**.
*   **Go Further:** Consider **smart light bulbs**.
    *   **Features:**
        *   Control lighting **remotely** via smartphone or tablet.
        *   Adjust **brightness and colour**.
        *   Monitor **energy usage** even when away from home.
