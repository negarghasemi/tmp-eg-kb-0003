# Canada Greener Homes Grant at a Glance

*Data as of June 2024*

## National Summary (Canada)

*   **Households completed retrofits and received a grant**: 239,696
*   **Applications being processed**: 220,932
*   **Average grant received per household**: $4,413
*   **Total grants issued to date**: $1.06B
*   **Homeowners choosing to install a heat pump**: 1 out of 2
*   **Heat pumps installed to date**: 135.9K
*   **Heat pumps expected by program end**: 250K
