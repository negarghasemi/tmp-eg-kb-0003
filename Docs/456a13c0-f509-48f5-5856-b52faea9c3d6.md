# Financial Help for Energy Efficiency

We're here to provide resources, rebates, loans, grants, and incentives to help you achieve an energy-efficient home. The Centre for Community Energy Transformation (CCET) offers free programs and resources to ensure you benefit from energy savings by removing cost barriers.

## Key Programs & Opportunities

### Federal Programs

#### Canada Greener Homes Loan
*   **Provider:** Canada Mortgage and Housing Corp. (CMHC)
*   **Offer:** Up to **$40,000** in **interest-free loans** for terms up to **10 years**.
*   **Details:** Supports eligible home energy retrofits.
*   **Learn more:** [Canada Greener Homes Loan](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/canada-greener-homes)

#### Federal Oil to Heat Pump Affordability Program
*   **Provider:** Natural Resources Canada
*   **Offer:** Up to **$10,000** to switch from oil heating to a new energy-efficient heat pump.
*   **Eligibility:**
    *   Home is heated by oil.
    *   Household income is at or below the median household after-tax income level.
*   **Eligible Measures:**
    *   Necessary electrical and mechanical upgrades for a new heat pump system.
    *   Installation of outdoor piping loops for ground source heat pump.
    *   Safe removal of oil tank.
    *   Installation of backup electrical heating (as required).
    *   Switching over other oil-using household systems, such as a water heater (where necessary).
*   **Learn more:** [Oil to Heat Pump Affordability Program](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/homes/canada-greener-homes-initiative/oil-heat-pump-affordability-program/24775)

### Utilities & Provincial Programs

#### Home Renovations Savings Program
*   **Partners:** Enbridge Gas and Save on Energy (with support from the Ontario Government)
*   **Purpose:** Helps Ontarians improve home energy efficiency and comfort.
*   **Available Rebates (require a Home Energy Assessment):**
    *   **Heat Pump Water Heaters:** $500
    *   **Windows and Doors:** $100 per rough opening
    *   **Insulation (attic, wall, foundation, exposed floor):** Up to $8,900
    *   **Air Sealing:** Up to $250
*   **Available Rebates (do not require a home energy assessment):**
    *   **Heat Pumps:** Up to $12,000
    *   **Air Source Heat Pumps (ASHP):** Up to $7,500
    *   **Solar Panels and Battery Storage:** Up to $10,000
    *   **Smart Thermostats:** $75
*   **ASHP Installation Requirements:** Must be an Enbridge Gas customer with an active account and primarily heat with natural gas furnace/boiler, OR be connected to the Ontario electricity grid and heat electrically. The installed heat pump must be on Natural Resources Canadas qualified products list.
*   **Learn more:** [Home Renovation Savings](https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/)

#### Save on Energy  Energy Affordability Program
*   **Provider:** Independent Electricity System Operator (IESO)
*   **Offer:** Expert advice and energy-saving home upgrades completely free of charge for income-eligible households.
*   **Eligibility:** Responsible for paying monthly electricity bill as a homeowner or renter. Eligibility depends on household income level from the previous year, household size, or receipt of a qualifying government/energy-support benefit. Residents of social housing may also be eligible.
*   **Types of Support:**
    *   [Comprehensive](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Energy-Affordability-Program#support)
    *   [Energy Saving Kits](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Energy-Affordability-Program#kits)
*   **Learn more:** [Energy Affordability Program](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Energy-Affordability-Program)

#### Enbridge Home Winterproofing Program (HWP)
*   **Provider:** Enbridge Gas
*   **Offer:** Free home energy upgrades (streamlined with the Energy Affordability Program).
*   **Eligibility:** If your home and household income levels qualify.
*   **Eligible Upgrades:** Free insulation, draft proofing, smart thermostat, Energy Star appliances, and more.
*   **Partnership:** CLEAResult is the authorized delivery partner. CCET is a community distribution partner.
*   **Contact:** Receive a free Energy Saving Kit by emailing [info@energytransform.ca](mailto:info@energytransform.ca).
*   **Learn more:** [Home Winterproofing Program](https://www.enbridgegas.com/residential/rebates-energy-conservation/home-winterproofing-program)

#### Hydro One Low Income Energy Assistance Program (LEAP)
*   **Provider:** Hydro One
*   **Offer:** Up to **$1,000** (or **$1,200** if electrically heated) towards your electricity bill.
*   **Learn more:** [Low-Income Energy Assistance Program](https://www.hydroone.com/saving-money-and-energy/residential/financial-assistance/low-income-assistance-program)

#### Ontario Electricity Support Program (OESP)
*   **Provider:** Ontario Energy Board (OEB)
*   **Offer:** Lowers electricity bills for lower-income households with a monthly credit.
*   **Credit Amount:** Depends on household income and size.
*   **Eligibility:** Customers of an electricity utility in a lower-income home.
*   **Learn more:** [Ontario Electricity Support Program](https://ontarioelectricitysupport.ca/)

### Loans & Financing

*We're updating financing options frequently, so check back for more opportunities.*

#### Canada Greener Homes Loan
*   **Provider:** Canada Mortgage and Housing Corp. (CMHC)
*   **Offer:** Up to **$40,000** in **interest-free loans** for terms up to **10 years**.
*   **Details:** See details at Natural Resources Canada site.
*   **Learn more:** [Canada Greener Homes Loan](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/canada-greener-homes)

#### Clean Energy Loan  RBC Energy Saver Loan
*   **Provider:** RBC Royal Bank
*   **Offer:** Available to RBC customers for purchasing hybrid cars, ENERGY STAR appliances, renewable energy projects, etc.
*   **Benefits:** Choose between a **1% interest rate reduction** or a **$100 rebate** on a clean energy audit with your loan.
*   **Learn more:** [RBC Energy Saver Loan](https://www.rbcroyalbank.com/personal-loans/energy-saver-loan.html)

#### Mortgage Insurance Loan Rebate
*   **Providers:** CMHC, Sagen, and Canada Guaranty
*   **Offer:** **25% mortgage loan insurance rebate** if you use a mortgage to buy a certified green home (e.g., R-2000, ENERGY STAR, Built Green, LEED).
*   **Learn more:** 
    *   [CMHC Eco Programs](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/consumers/home-buying/mortgage-loan-insurance-for-consumers/cmhc-eco-programs/cmhc-eco-plus)
    *   [Sagen Energy Efficient Housing](https://www.sagen.ca/products-and-services/energy-efficient-housing/)
    *   [Canada Guaranty Energy-Efficient Advantage Program](https://www.canadaguaranty.ca/energy-efficient-advantage-program/)

#### Canada Green Affordable Housing Program
*   **Provider:** CMHC
*   **Offer:** Funding for retrofits for homeowners meeting affordability criteria.
*   **Funding Details:** Up to **100% of eligible retrofit costs** to a maximum of **$170,000 per unit**; forgivable loans will be either **$85,000 per unit** or **80% of eligible costs**.
*   **Process:** Homeowners must apply for funding and carry out pre-retrofit activities (e.g., energy audits, energy modeling studies).
*   **Learn more:** [Canada Green Affordable Housing Program](https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/professionals/project-funding-and-mortgage-financing/funding-programs/all-funding-programs/canada-greener-affordable-housing-program)

### Rebates & Incentives

#### Hydro One - Ontario Electricity Rebate (OER)
*   **Offer:** **13.1% reduction** on your electricity bill (before HST), as of November 1, 2024.
*   **Automatic Eligibility:**
    *   Residential and Small Commercial customers (billed under a Residential or General Service Energy rate class) using less than 50 kW.
    *   Commercial accounts greater than 50 kW but using less than 250,000 kWh annually.
*   **Learn more:** [Ontario Electricity Rebate](https://www.hydroone.com/business-services/commercial-industrial-generators-and-ldcs/commercial-industrial-customers/ontario-electricity-rebate#:~:text=The%20Ontario%20Electricity%20Rebate%20(%E2%80%9COER,as%20of%20November%201%2C%202023.)

#### Enbridge Fixed Incentive Program
*   **Purpose:** Offsets the cost of upgrading to energy-efficient natural gas technology.
*   **Process:** Energy Solutions Advisor helps identify opportunities, quantify savings, and provide incentives.
*   **Learn more:** [Fixed Incentive Program](https://www.enbridgegas.com/business-industrial/incentives-conservation/programs-and-incentives/equipment-upgrades/fixed-incentive-program)

#### Enbridge Business Programs and Incentives
*   **Offer:** Rebates and incentives for equipment upgrades, new construction, retrofits, and energy assessments.
*   **Learn more:** [Enbridge Business Programs and Incentives](https://www.enbridgegas.com/business-industrial/incentives-conservation/programs-and-incentives)

## Income Qualified FREE Programs

#### Federal Oil to Heat Pump Affordability Program
*   **Offer:** Up to **$10,000** to switch from oil heating to a new energy-efficient heat pump.
*   **Eligibility:** Home heated by oil, household income at or below median after-tax income.

#### 100% Free Cold Climate Air Source Heat Pumps
*   **Provider:** Save on Energy (through its Energy Affordability Program)
*   **Offer:** Free cold climate air source heat pumps for qualifying Ontario households in electrically heated homes.

*   **Additional Eligibility:** May qualify for other free energy efficiency upgrades like ENERGY STAR appliances (refrigerators, freezers, window AC, lighting), weatherstripping, attic/basement insulation, smart thermostats, and power strips.
