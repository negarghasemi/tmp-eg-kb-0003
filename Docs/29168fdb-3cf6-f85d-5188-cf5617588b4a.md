# Heating with Electricity

This document, produced by **Natural Resources Canada's Office of Energy Efficiency**, is part of the **EnerGuide** Heating and Cooling Series.

## About EnerGuide
*EnerGuide is the official Government of Canada mark associated with the labelling and rating of the energy consumption or energy efficiency of household appliances, heating and ventilation equipment, air conditioners, houses and vehicles.*

EnerGuide's purpose is to:
*   Help manufacturers and dealers promote energy-efficient equipment.
*   Provide consumers with the necessary information to choose energy-efficient residential equipment.
