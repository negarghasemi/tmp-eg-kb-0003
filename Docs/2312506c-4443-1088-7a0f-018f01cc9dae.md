# Clothes washers (and household-style commercial)

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition
**Clothes washer**: Electrically operated clothes washer that does not require mechanical fastening to a floor or wall, is top- or front- loaded and has an internal control system that regulates the water temperature without the need for user intervention after the machine is set in operation.

### Compliance Date
Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2018**
*For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations*

### Compliant Product Models
- [Clothes washers](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=CLOTHESDRYERS&page=1)
- [Household-style commercial clothes washers](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=ClothesWashers.Comm)

*As listed on NRCans searchable database.*

### Labelling Requirements
EnerGuide label required on **household clothes washers** and voluntary on **household-style commercial clothes washers**.

### Testing Standard
**CAN/CSA-C360-13**

### Energy Efficiency Standard

**Energy performance standard for clothes washers**

#### Household clothes washers

| Product class            | Minimum integrated modified energy factor L/kWh/cycle | Maximum integrated water factor L/cycle/L |
| :----------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- |
| **Vertical-axis compact**  | 32.56                                                 | 1.60                                      |
| **Vertical-axis standard** | 44.46                                                 | 0.87                                      |
| **Horizontal-axis compact**| 32.00                                                 | 1.11                                      |
| **Horizontal-axis standard**| 52.10                                                 | 0.63                                      |

#### Household-style commercial clothes washers

| Product class     | Minimum modified energy factor L/kWh/cycle | Maximum integrated water consumption factor L/cycle/L |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Vertical-axis** | 38.23                                      | 1.18                                                  |
| **Horizontal-axis**| 56.63                                      | 0.55                                                  |

*Units:
- **L/kWh/cycle**: litres per kilowatt-hour per cycle
- **L/cycle/L**: litres per cycle per litre*

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements
- **Name of product**
- **Brand name**
- **Model number**
- **Manufacturer**
- The name of the **certification body or province** whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
- Whether a **mathematical model** as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
- **Household**: integrated modified energy factor in L/kWh/cycle
- **Household-style commercial**: modified energy factor in L/kWh/cycle
- **Annual energy consumption** in kWh per year
- **Size category**: compact or standard
- **Capacity**, in Litres, of clothes washers container
- **Integrated water consumption factor** in litres per cycle per litre
- **Maximum wash temperature available**: less than or equal to 57.2C or greater than 57.2C
- **Wash rinse temperature selections** listed in Table 5 of CAN/CSA-C360-13
- **Water fill control system**: manual or adaptive
- **Rotational axis of clothes washer container**: horizontal or vertical
- **Household-style commercial**: method for payment, if any
