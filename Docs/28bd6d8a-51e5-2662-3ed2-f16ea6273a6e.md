# Keeping the Heat In

Natural Resources Canada's Office of Energy Efficiency offers this guide to educate on basic principles of building science and to provide guidance in home retrofit projects such as insulation and air sealing improvements.

## Chapter Summary

- **Section 1**: Introduction
- **Section 2**: How your house works
- **Section 3**: Materials *(insulation, house wrap, barriers, weatherstripping)*
- **Section 4**: Comprehensive air leakage control
- **Section 5**: Roofs and attics
- **Section 6**: Basement insulation
- **Section 7**: Insulating Walls *(insulating, renovating, building additions)*
- **Section 8**: Upgrading windows and doors
- **Section 9**: Operating your house *(thermostats, water heaters, furnaces, other heating/cooling systems)*
