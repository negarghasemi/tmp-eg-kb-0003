# Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment

## Non-Residential EV Supply Equipment Connections

Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) connections include, but are not limited to, non-residential customer applications such as Level 2 and Level 3 charging stations. This covers publicly accessible direct current fast charging stations, workplace charging, charging stations for commercial EV fleets, and charging installations for multi-unit residential or commercial buildings where EV chargers are owned or operated by the building owner or a third-party charging provider.

*For detailed information regarding Electric Vehicle Charging Connection Procedure (EVCCP), please refer to Appendix 1 of the [Conditions of Service](/media/5306/download?attachment).* 

### Preliminary Consultation (Optional)

For non-residential EV charging connections (Level 2 and above), typically servicing fleet connections or multiple charging facilities:

-   Fill out the optional preliminary consultation information request: [*EVPCIR Form*](https://www.oakvillehydro.com/sites/oakvillehydro/files/2024-05/Oakville%20Hydro_EVCCP_EVPCIR%20and%20EVPCR%20Form_0.XLSX)
-   Submit the form to: *engineering@oakvillehydro.com*
-   Oakville Hydro will respond within **15 calendar days** to confirm if the EVPCIR form is complete or if additional information is required.

### Connection Request

To connect an EVSE to Oakville Hydros grid, a commercial connection request application must be submitted by following these steps:

1.  Visit the [Forms and Information page](https://www.oakvillehydro.com/accounts-customer-services/forms-and-information) and scroll down to the new connections and upgrades heading.
2.  Fill out and submit a form for either **primary** or **secondary new connection**.

**Response Timelines:**

-   Oakville Hydro will respond within **15 calendar days** to confirm if the connection request is complete or if additional information is required.
-   Once the connection request submission is deemed complete, Oakville Hydro will provide an offer to connect within **45 calendar days**.

### Important Note

This process is **NOT applicable** to EV chargers installed by individual residential customers or unit owners/tenants of a multi-unit residential building.
