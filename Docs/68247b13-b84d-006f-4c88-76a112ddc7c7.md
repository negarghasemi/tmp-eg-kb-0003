# Home Renovation Savings Program: No Energy Assessment Required Upgrades

This program offers rebates for energy-efficient home improvements without requiring a prior home energy assessment.

## Program Overview
It's now easier to get rebates for the following upgrades:
- **Heat pumps**
- **Solar panels**
- **Attic insulation**
- **Smart thermostats**
- **Appliances**

## Available Rebates and Upgrades

### Cold-climate Air Source or Ground Source Heat Pumps
- **Rebate**: Up to **$12,000***, depending on your homes primary space heating energy source.
- *Process*: Work with a program-qualified contractor for installation and rebate paperwork.
- [See rebates details](/without-assessment/heat-pumps)

### Solar Panels Plus Battery Storage
- **Rebate**: Up to **$10,000***
    - Up to **$5,000*** for solar panels.
    - Additional rebate up to **$5,000*** for bundling with battery storage.
- *Process*: Choose your own contractor (no program-qualified list).
- [See rebates details](/without-assessment/solar)

### Attic Insulation
- **Rebate**: Up to **$1,250***
    - Up to **$1,250*** for attic insulation.
    - Up to **$750*** for cathedral ceiling or flat roof insulation.
- *Process*: Work with a program-qualified contractor for installation and rebate paperwork.
- [See rebates details](/without-assessment/attic)

### Smart Thermostats
- **Rebate**: **$100*** off an energy-saving smart thermostat.
- *Process*: Easily purchase and install on your own (before or after purchase options available).
- [See rebates details](/without-assessment/smart-thermostat)

### Appliances
- **Rebate**: Up to **$200*** for upgrading to qualified energy-efficient appliances.
- *Process*: Easily purchase and install on your own.
- [See rebates details](/without-assessment/appliances)

## General Information

### Saving Energy Made Easier and More Affordable
- For **heat pumps** and **attic insulation**, work with a program-qualified contractor. They will handle the rebate paperwork.
- For **solar panels**, you need to choose your own contractor.
- For **smart thermostats** and **appliances**, you can purchase and install them yourself.

### Bundled Options
- If you're looking to do **more than one upgrade**, bundled options are available for maximum energy savings.
- [See upgrades requiring an energy assessment](/with-assessment)
