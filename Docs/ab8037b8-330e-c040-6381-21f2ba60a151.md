# Ductless Heating and Cooling: Energy Efficiency Report

## Overview

**ENERGY STAR certified ductless heating and cooling systems** offer significant energy savings and improved comfort without the need for bulky ductwork. Systems earning the **ENERGY STAR Most Efficient** designation provide the best performance.

## Key Energy Savings & Features

*   **Heating Cost Reduction**: Cut your electrical heating bill by **60%** compared to conventional electric resistance-based heating systems.
*   **Cooling Cost Reduction**: Cool for **30% less** compared to conventional room air conditioners.
*   **Advanced Technology**: Utilizes advanced compressors and fans that adjust speeds to optimize energy use.
*   **Zoned Comfort**: Each indoor unit can provide custom heating and cooling, adjustable via wall consoles, remote controls, and smartphone apps.
*   **Easy Installation**: Connects indoor units to an outdoor unit using small refrigerant lines, requiring only a three-inch hole in an outdoor wall. Up to 8 indoor units can be connected to a single outdoor unit.

## When to Consider Upgrading

Ductless mini-split heat pumps are ideal for:

*   Homes with costly electric heating and cooling (e.g., baseboard, electric radiant, furnace).
*   Older homes lacking ductwork.
*   Homes with high central heating costs due to expensive fuel.
*   Rooms that are not regularly occupied, allowing for individual temperature control.
*   Spaces adjacent to unconditioned areas where ductwork would be exposed (e.g., above a garage).
*   Additions or outbuildings where extending existing ductwork is not feasible (e.g., shed, barn, garage).

## Product Labels and Standards

*   **ENERGY STAR Symbol**: Indicates the model is highly energy efficient.
*   **EnerGuide Label**: Shows the model's **Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio (SEER)** or cooling efficiency and allows for comparison with similar models.

## Future Considerations for Cold Climates

While heat pumps traditionally work harder in colder temperatures, newer models feature **advanced compressors and refrigerants** that significantly improve low-temperature performance. For optimal performance in colder climates, look for:

*   ENERGY STAR models with a **Heating Seasonal Performance Factor (HSPF) of over 12.0 BTU/Wh4**.
*   Consult the Northeast Energy Efficiency Partnerships (NEEP) list of cold climate ductless mini-split heat pumps.

## The ENERGY STAR Promise

All ENERGY STAR certified products are:

*   Tested to meet strict efficiency specifications.
*   Certified by an independent third party.
*   Guaranteed to perform the same as or better than standard products without compromising performance.

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

Ductless heat pumps are subject to **Canada's *Energy Efficiency Regulations***, which mandate minimum performance standards for energy efficiency. These regulations categorize ductless heat pumps under \"split-system central air conditioners and heat pumps.\"
