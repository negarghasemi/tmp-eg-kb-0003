# Save on Energy Resources

Oakville Hydro is committed to helping customers manage energy use and lower electricity costs through smart, reliable solutions. They provide access to programs, incentives, and tools that support energy efficiency in partnership with the Independent Electricity System Operator (IESO).

## Available Resources

*   [**Save on Energy**](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/) *offers a range of programs for both residential and small business customers.*
*   [**The Ontario Minister of Energy**](https://www.ontario.ca/page/how-use-less-electricity-home) *provides tips on how to reduce electricity use and costs at home.*
*   [**Energy Star**](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products) *makes it easy for consumers and businesses to save money and protect the environment.*

## Save on Energy Programs for Homeowners

### Home Renovation Savings Program

*   **Description**: Enbridge Gas and Save on Energy, with support from the Ontario Government, have partnered to launch the new Home Renovation Savings program to help Ontarians improve home energy efficiency and comfort. Receive rebates on energy-efficient home upgrades, as well as for energy efficient appliances.
*   **Learn More**: [https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/](https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/)

### Peak Perks

*   **Description**: Get $75 and help reduce summer energy use! Join Peak Perks and let Save on Energy adjust your smart thermostat on hot afternoons. Plus, earn $20 every year you stay in. You remain in control at all times and can opt out whenever you choose.
*   **Learn More**: [https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Peak-Perks](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Peak-Perks)

### Energy Affordability Program

*   **Description**: The Energy Affordability Program provides income-eligible households with no-cost support to help manage electricity use, reduce monthly energy costs, and improve home comfort. Depending on eligibility, support may include a free home energy assessment with a trained energy-efficiency expert, as well as energy-saving upgrades such as insulation, LED lighting, energy-efficient appliances, and smart or programmable thermostats.
*   **Learn More**: [https://www.saveonenergy.ca/EAP](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/EAP)

*View all financial incentives and programs for your home*: [https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home)

## Save on Energy Programs for Small Businesses

### Small Business Program

*   **Description**: Small businesses with an operating capacity of 50 employees or fewer can make equipment improvements to reduce their energy consumption at no cost. Save your small business money with Save on Energy incentives of up to $3,000 for eligible lighting equipment and up to $2,500 for eligible non-lighting equipment.
*   **Learn More**: [http://www.saveonenergy.ca/sbp](http://www.saveonenergy.ca/sbp)

### Retrofit Program

*   **Description**: Get financial incentives to upgrade equipment that will lower energy costs, improve productivity and support your businesss sustainability goals. Get up to 50% of eligible project costs covered for targeted energy-efficiency retrofits. Applicants are required to submit an application and receive pre-approval for their projects to participate in the Retrofit program.
*   **Learn More**: [https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Business-and-Industry/Programs-and-incentives/Retrofit-Program](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Business-and-Industry/Programs-and-incentives/Retrofit-Program)

### Instant Discounts Program

*   **Description**: Receive upfront discounts from participating distributors on the purchase of energy-efficient lighting products that will help to lower your businesss energy consumption, improve your bottom line and transform your space.
*   **Learn More**: [https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Business-and-Industry/Programs-and-incentives/Instant-Discounts-Program](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Business-and-Industry/Programs-and-incentives/Instant-Discounts-Program)

### Peak Perks (Small Business)

*   **Description**: Get $75 and help reduce summer energy use! Join Peak Perks and let Save on Energy adjust your smart thermostat on hot afternoons. Plus, earn $20 every year you stay in. You remain in control at all times and can opt out whenever you choose.
*   **Learn More**: [https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Peak-Perks](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Peak-Perks)

*View all financial incentives and programs for your small business*: [https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Small-Business/Programs-and-Incentives](https://saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Small-Business/Programs-and-Incentives)
