# Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED)

*An initiative by Natural Resources Canada (NRCan), last modified on 2025-12-23.*

![Energy efficiency products: Kitchen with a fridge; a man charging an electric car; a wall thermostat; a clothes washer and dryer](/sites/nrcan/files/oee/images/energystar/images/EnergyEfficiency_Products.png)

## Overview

The **Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED)** is a secure online system developed by Natural Resources Canada's (NRCan) Compliance Operations Group (COG). Its purpose is to simplify and streamline the submission of compliance and energy efficiency information by dealers of regulated energy-using products and authorized certification bodies. The system provides pre-defined templates for creating, submitting, and managing energy efficiency reports.

## Modernization Benefits

CREED aims to reduce the workload for dealers, certification bodies, and NRCan by:

*   **Automating processes**
*   **Validating submitted data in near real-time**
*   **Reducing administrative time and back-and-forth communication**
*   **Increasing productivity**
*   **Providing useful digital tools and better functionality**
*   **Aligning with provincial and international partners**

Users will be able to:

*   Upload submissions directly.
*   Create, submit, and manage reports using product or equipment-specific templates.
*   Access previous submissions.
*   Track submission status with automated messages.
*   View reports and statistics.
*   Ask questions to program staff.
*   Run searches on submitted reports.

## Upcoming Access to CREED for Submitting Compliance Reports

The new system began staged releases in **2024** and is rolling out in several phases with dedicated support for stakeholders.

## Free Trade and Labour Mobility in Canada Act (FTLMCA) Reporting Requirements

FTLMCA-certified provincial compliance **will not** be processed through CREED. Regulatees must submit necessary compliance documents directly to NRCan via email. Refer to the [Guidance for complying with the Regulations using the Free Trade and Labour Mobility in Canada Act](/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/compliance-free-trade-labour-mobility-canada-act) for details.

## Canadas Energy Efficiency Act and Energy Efficiency Regulations

All regulated products must meet federal energy efficiency standards for import into Canada or inter-provincial shipment for sale or lease. These Regulations also apply if products are incorporated into larger units or machines, even if the larger unit is unregulated.
