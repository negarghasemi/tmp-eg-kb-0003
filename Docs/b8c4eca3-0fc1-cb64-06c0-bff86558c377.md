# Room air conditioners  Upcoming Requirements

*The testing standard and energy efficiency standard will apply to product manufactured on or after May 26, 2026. All other requirements in effect October 9, 2025.*

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition

**Room air conditioner**: A single-phase electric air conditioner that has a cooling capacity of **10.55 kilowatts (36,000 Btu/h)** or less. It does not include a packaged terminal air conditioner, a portable air conditioner, or a single package vertical air conditioner.

### Compliance Date

*Manufactured on or after* **May 26, 2026**

*For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations.*

### Labelling

**EnerGuide label required**. The label must be printed on, or affixed to, the principal display panel of the products package. _(See Schedule 3 for details)._

### Testing Standard

**Technical standards document D2SA001** entitled *Testing Standard and Energy Efficiency Standards for Room Air Conditioners*.

### Energy Efficiency Regulations by Province

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements

Reports must include the following information:

-   **Name of product**
-   **Brand name**
-   **Model number**
-   **Manufacturer**
-   **Name of the certification body** or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
-   Whether a **mathematical model** as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
-   **Cooling capacity**, in kW (Btu/h)
-   **CEER**
-   **Product type**
