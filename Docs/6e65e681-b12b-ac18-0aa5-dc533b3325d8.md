# General Service Lamps Energy Efficiency Report

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition

**General service lamp** means an electrical device that is used in general lighting applications and that:

-   has a base that complies with ANSI C81.61;
-   is able to operate, in the case of an integrated lamp, at
    -   a voltage of 12 V or 24V,
    -   a voltage ranging from 100 V to 130 V,
    -   a voltage ranging from 220 V to 240 V, or
    -   a voltage of 277 V or 347 V;
-   is able to operate at any voltage, in the case of a non-integrated lamp; and
-   has an initial lumen output of
    -   at least **310 lm but no more than 3300 lm**, or
    -   in the case of a modified spectrum incandescent lamp, at least **232 lm but no more than 3300 lm**.

**Includes:**

-   compact fluorescent lamps
-   general service incandescent lamps;
-   general service incandescent reflector lamps;
-   general service light-emitting diode (LED) lamps, and
-   general service organic light-emitting diode (OLED) lamps

**Does not include:**

-   a light fixture;
-   an LED downlight retrofit kit;
-   an appliance lamp;
-   a black light lamp;
-   a bug lamp;
-   a coloured lamp;
-   a G-shape lamp, as described in ANSI C78.79, with a diameter of 127 mm (5 inches) or more;
-   a high-intensity discharge lamp;
-   an infrared lamp;
-   a J-, JC-, JCD-, JCS-, JCV-, JCX-, JD-, JS- or JT-shape lamp that does not have an Edison screw base as defined in ANSI C81.61;
-   a lamp that has a wedge base or pre-focus base;
-   a left-hand thread lamp;
-   a marine lamp;
-   a marine signal service lamp;
-   a mine service lamp;
-   an MR-shape lamp that has a first number symbol equal to 16 (diameter equal to 51 mm (2 inches)), as described in ANSI C78.79, operates at 12 V and has a lumen output greater than or equal to 800 lm;
-   a fluorescent lamp other than a CFL;
-   a plant lamp;
-   an R20 short lamp;
-   a reflector lamp that
    -   has a first number symbol less than 16 (diameter less than 51 mm (2 inches)), as described in ANSI C78.79, and
    -   does not have an E26/E24, E26d, E26/50x39, E26/53x39, E29/28, E29/53x39, E39, E39d, EP39 or EX39 base;
-   an S-shape or G-shape lamp that has a first number symbol less than or equal to 12.5 (diameter less than or equal to 40 mm (1.5625 inches)), as described in ANSI C78.79;
-   a sign service lamp;
-   a silver bowl lamp;
-   a showcase lamp;
-   a specialty MR lamp;
-   a T-shape lamp, other than a CFL, that
    -   has a first number symbol less than or equal to 8 (diameter less than or equal to 25 mm (1 inch)), as described in ANSI C78.79, and
    -   has a nominal overall length less than 305 mm (12 inches); or
-   a traffic signal lamp.

### Compliance Date

-   **Manufactured on or after January 1st, 2026** for general service incandescent lamps or a general service incandescent reflector lamps.
-   **Manufactured on or after January 1st, 2027** for other general service lamps.

_For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations._

### Early Compliance

A general service lamp is deemed to satisfy the requirements of sections 4, 5, 435 and 436 if:

-   it was manufactured on or after **January 1, 2025 and before January 1, 2027** and
-   satisfies the requirements of the sections that would have applied to the lamp if it had been manufactured on or after January 1, 2027.

### Labelling Requirements

General service lamps manufactured on or after **January 1, 2027** must have a principal display panel on the products package that displays the following information in the following order:

-   The words **Light output** and **Flux lumineux**, followed by the numerical value of the products luminous flux and the word lumens;
-   The words **Efficacy** and **Efficacit**, followed by the numerical value of the products nominal efficacy and the words lumens/watt;
-   The words **Power** and **Puissance**, followed by the numerical value of the products nominal power consumption and the word watts;
-   The words **Life** and **Dure de vie**, followed by the numerical value of the products life and the words hours and heures, respectively; and
-   The words **Light appearance** and **Apparence de la lumire**, followed by the numerical value of the products correlated colour temperature and the upper case letter K or the word Kelvin.

**Additional Label Requirements:**

-   Have the words Light output, Flux lumineux, Efficacy, Efficacit, Power, Puissance, Life, Dure de vie, Light appearance and Apparence de la lumire be in the same font and be equal in size.
-   Have the words lumens, lumens/watt, watts, hours, heures and Kelvin and the upper case letter K be in the same font and be equal in size, but not be more than 50% of the size of the words referred to above.
-   The numerical values must be in the same font and size.
-   If the product is a three-way lamp, the information required must be displayed for each level of the lamps operation.
-   If the product has a variable correlated colour temperature, the information must be displayed for the lamps range of operation or for each level of its operation.
-   The lamp model number must be indicated on both the product packaging and the lamp itself.

_NRCan offers [label design templates](/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/general-service-lamp-labelling-design-templates) that dealers can use to meet the GSL labelling requirements. Please note, that using the NRCan design template is not mandatory._

### Testing Standard

Paragraph **430.23(gg) of Subpart B, Part 430 of Title 10 to the U.S. Code of Federal Regulations**, entitled General Service Lamps.

### Energy Efficiency Standard

All general service lamps defined in the Regulations must meet a minimum efficacy of **45 lumens per watt (lm/W)** per the energy efficiency standards set out in [TSD D7SB001 (PDF, 323 Ko)](/sites/admin/files/documents/2025-03/TSD%20D7SB001%20revision%2000%20%28ENGLISH%29.pdf).

### Energy Efficiency Report

The energy efficiency report required for GSLs would include the following product specific information:

-   a description of the lamp, collected in accordance with ANSI C78.79,
-   the rated lumen output or, in the case of a three-way lamp, the three rated lumen outputs, in lumens,
-   the rated power or, in the case of a three-way lamp, the three values for rated power, in watts,
-   the rated lamp efficacy, in lm/W,
-   the rated life, in hours,
-   the rated correlated colour temperature or range of correlated colour temperatures, in degrees Kelvin, and
-   the rated colour rendering index.

_Sign in to the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED)](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/) to download and submit an energy efficiency report for this product._
