# Room Air Conditioners - Energy Efficiency Information

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition
**Room air conditioner**: A single-phase electric air conditioner with a cooling capacity of **10.55 kilowatts (36,000 Btu/h)** or less. This excludes packaged terminal air conditioners, portable air conditioners, and single package vertical air conditioners.

### Compliance Date
Manufactured on or after **June 1, 2014**
_For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations._

### Compliant Product Models
[Room air conditioners](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=AC.Room&page=1) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Labelling Requirements
**EnerGuide label required**. The label must be printed on, or affixed to, the principal display panel of the products package. (See [Schedule 3](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2016-311/page-38.html#h-837657)).

### Testing Standard
**CSA-C368.1-14**

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements
To download and submit an energy efficiency report, sign in to the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED)](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/). The report must include:

- **Name of product**
- **Brand name**
- **Model number**
- **Manufacturer**
- **Name of the certification body or province** whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
- Whether a **mathematical model** as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
- **Cooling capacity**, in **kW (Btu/h)**
- **CEER**
- **Product type**
