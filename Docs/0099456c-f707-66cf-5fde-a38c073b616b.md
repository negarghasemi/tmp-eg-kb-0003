# Other products

## ENERGY STAR(R) certified products save energy, save money and protect the planet

When Canadians think about ENERGY STAR, they usually think about appliances, electronics, lighting, or heating/cooling equipment. However, there are other products that fall into a category of their own.

The following ENERGY STAR certified products help Canadians save energy at home, at work, and beyond:

- [Electrical vehicle (EV) chargers](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products/electric-vehicle-chargers)
- [Lab fridges and freezers](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products/lab-fridges-freezers)
- [Pool pumps](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products/pool-pumps)
- [Water coolers](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products/water-coolers)

### Didnt find what you were looking for?

[See the list of ENERGY STAR certified products in Canada](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products).

Although certified in the United States, the following products are not eligible for certification in Canada, due to climate differences:

- Roofing products
- Sealing and Insulation products
