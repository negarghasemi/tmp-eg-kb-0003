# Energy Efficiency Report: Electric Ranges

This report outlines the energy efficiency regulations for **electric ranges** in Canada.

## Regulatory Details

*   **Regulatory Definition**: An electric range is defined as a household electric range. It specifically *excludes* portable ranges designed for a 120 V electrical supply and microwave ovens.
*   **Compliance Date**: Products manufactured on or after **August 1, 2003**, must comply with these regulations. *For models manufactured prior to this date, refer to the specific Regulations.* 
*   **Labelling Requirements**: An **EnerGuide label** is required for compliant products.
*   **Testing Standard**: The required testing standard is **CAN/CSA-C358-03**.

## Energy Performance Standard

The following table outlines the maximum allowable energy consumption for different types of electric ranges:

| Product Type | Maximum Allowable Energy Consumption (kWh/year) |
| :----------- | :---------------------------------------------- |
| **Ranges**   | **2.0V + 458**                                  |
| **Cooktops** | **258**                                         |
| **Ovens**    | **2.0V + 200**                                  |

*Where:* 
- **V** = oven volume, in Litres
- **kWh/year** = kilowatt-hours per year

## Energy Efficiency Report Requirements

When submitting an energy efficiency report for an electric range, the following information must be included:

- **Name of product**
- **Brand name**
- **Model number**
- **Manufacturer**
- **Name of the certification body or province** whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
- Indication of **whether a mathematical model** (as defined in the Regulations) was used to generate any of the provided information
- **Annual energy consumption** in kWh
- **Type and size category**
- **Volume**, in litres, of usable oven space for each oven
- **Exterior oven width**
- **Oven configuration** (e.g., single oven, double oven, or double oven with microwave upper oven)
- **Baking mode** the product has (e.g., normal bake or normal bake with forced convection)
- Information indicating whether the product is **built-in or wall-mounted**
- **Annual clock energy consumption** in kWh

*This information is to be submitted via the Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED).*
