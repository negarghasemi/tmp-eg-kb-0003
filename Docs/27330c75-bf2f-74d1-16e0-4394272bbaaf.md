# Peak Perks Program

*A program designed to reward participants for reducing energy use during peak demand periods, supporting Ontario's electricity grid.* 

## Program Overview

Peak Perks is Canadas fastest-growing demand response program, involving over **300,000 participants**. It revolutionizes how Ontario powers up by enabling smart thermostat adjustments during high electricity demand, primarily on hot summer days. This collective effort has saved enough energy to supply an entire city the size of **Oshawa**.

## Benefits and Incentives

### For You

*   **Enrollment Incentive**: **$75** virtual prepaid Mastercard when you sign up.
*   **Annual Incentive**: **$20** virtual prepaid Mastercard for each year you remain enrolled.
*   **Energy Savings**: Reduce your energy consumption at home or work.

### For Your Community

*   Help reduce demand on Ontarios electricity grid.
*   Support reliable and affordable energy for everyone.

## How Peak Perks Works

Participants with eligible smart thermostats allow brief, time-limited adjustments to their thermostat settings during periods of peak electricity demand. 

### Adjustment Process

*   **Before an Adjustment**: Your thermostat may be adjusted down slightly to pre-cool your home or business, ensuring comfort during the event.
*   **During an Adjustment**: Your thermostat will be automatically adjusted by **no more than two degrees Celsius**, for up to **three hours**.
*   **After an Adjustment**: Your thermostat will automatically return to its original temperature setting.

### Your Control

*   You will be **notified before every adjustment**.
*   You can **opt out at any time** by manually adjusting your thermostat or using your providers mobile app.

## Eligibility

To participate in the Peak Perks program, you must meet the following criteria:

*   **Residential Customers**: Be an electricity customer in Ontario with central air conditioning or a heat pump that is controlled by your smart thermostat.
*   **Business Customers**: Be a business with **50 or fewer employees** on-site at any point in time.
*   **Smart Thermostat**: Have an eligible Wi-Fi enabled smart thermostat installed in your home or business.
*   **Program Exclusivity**: Not be participating in another demand response program in Ontario where your thermostat is automatically adjusted to maintain grid reliability.
    *   *Exception*: Residents of Sarnia participating in the Enbridge Gas Sarnia Saves program are also eligible for Peak Perks.
*   **Geographic Exclusion**: Electricity customers in **Cornwall, Ontario**, are **not eligible** as they are connected to the Hydro Quebec electricity grid.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Program Overview

*   **How to Sign Up**: Visit the program enrollment page, answer questions, and select your thermostat provider. You can also enroll via your thermostat providers mobile app or directly on your thermostat. You will receive an email notification upon enrollment confirmation.
*   **Notification of Adjustment**: You may receive notifications via your smart thermostat app or on the thermostat itself before an event begins.
*   **Compromising Comfort**: You are always in control and can opt out at any time from your mobile device or by manually adjusting your thermostat.
*   **Opting Out**: Yes, you can opt out directly from your thermostat or the manufacturers app during an event.
*   **Third-Party Involvement**: Save on Energy works with EnergyHub, Choice Digital, and Blackhawk Network to administer the program and incentives. Communications may come from these parties.
*   **Why Participate**: Get financial rewards, help manage electricity consumption, and contribute to a reliable, affordable, and sustainable grid in Ontario.

### Residential Enrollment

*   **Condo/Apartment Eligibility**: Low-rise single-family units with dedicated independent AC are eligible. Units in high-rise buildings using large central HVAC are not.
*   **Renters Eligibility**: Yes, renters can participate if all other program requirements are met.
*   **Moving Homes**: If you move and reconnect your thermostat, you will not receive an additional enrollment incentive but will continue to receive the annual incentive for continued participation.
*   **Multiple Properties**: You may enroll multiple eligible properties (each with a separate AC system) that you own.
*   **Thermostats per Address**: For residential addresses, the incentive is capped at **three devices**. A fourth device can enroll but will not receive incentives.
*   **Peak Perks vs. Hydro One myEnergy Rewards**: Peak Perks is available to residential and small business customers across Ontario, while myEnergy Rewards is only for Hydro One residential customers. Customers can choose which program to join; they cannot participate in both with the same thermostat.

### Business Enrollment

*   **Application Name**: Please use your business name as the first name on the Peak Perks application.
*   **Thermostat Replacement**: If you replace your thermostat, you will continue participation but will not receive a new enrollment incentive. You will still receive the annual incentive.
*   **Multiple Business Properties**: You may enroll multiple business properties (each with a separate AC system) that you own.

### Incentives

*   **Virtual Prepaid Mastercard**: Your virtual prepaid Mastercard will be sent to the email address used for registration from `peakperks@redeem.energyhub.com`. It expires within **six months** of issuance.
*   **Physical Card Option**: A physical prepaid card can be requested by contacting `support@cdco.io`.
*   **My Prepaid Center Issues**: For difficulties accessing My Prepaid Center to redeem your Mastercard, contact them at **1-888-371-2109**.
*   **Incentive Cap for Businesses**: There is no incentive cap for small businesses; all eligible smart thermostats connected to separate AC systems qualify for incentives.

### Support

*   **Data Collection and Privacy**: The program collects smart thermostat data and energy use in accordance with terms and conditions. Personal information is protected, and informed consent is obtained. Data is accessible to IESO and its authorized partner, EnergyHub.
*   **Direct Solicitation**: Save on Energy **does not** call, contact, or visit homes to solicit participation. Report any fraudulent activity to `saveonenergy@ieso.ca`.
*   **Additional Questions**: For more information, email `info@peakperks.ca` or call **(877) 793-7375**.
*   **Unenrollment**: To unenroll, send an email to `info@peakperks.ca`. Upon unenrollment, your thermostat will no longer participate in activations, and you will not receive the annual participation incentive.
