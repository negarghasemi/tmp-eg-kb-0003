# Regulatory announcements

## Amendments to the *Energy Efficiency Regulations*

Amendments to Canadas *Energy Efficiency Regulations* typically follow this public consultation process:

1.  **Initial notice**: Natural Resources Canadas intent to proceed with the development of an amendment to the Regulations is posted in the [Forward Regulatory Plan](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/corporate/transparency/acts-regulations/forward-regulatory-plan) for the *Energy Efficiency Regulations*.
2.  **Pre-consultation**: technical bulletins and webinars are posted on the Natural Resources Canada website when the proposed changes would impact Canadians or regulated parties (see [current regulatory proposals](#current-regulatory-proposals)).
3.  **Pre-publication**: the proposed regulatory amendment is pre-published in the *Canada Gazette*, Part I for a public comment period when the proposed changes would impact Canadians or regulated parties.
4.  **Publication**: the final version of the regulatory amendment, including revisions and coming into force dates, is published in the *Canada Gazette*, Part II (see [published regulations](#published-regulations)).
