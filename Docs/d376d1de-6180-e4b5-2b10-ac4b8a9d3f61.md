# Customer Choice Rules for Electricity Pricing

This document outlines the rules for switching electricity pricing plans with Oakville Hydro, specifically focusing on moving to Tiered prices.

## Key Information for Switching Electricity Pricing Plans

### 1. Purpose
*   To switch to **Tiered prices** or other available electricity pricing plans.

### 2. Required Action: Filling out the Election Form
*   **Form**: Complete the [election form](https://www.oakvillehydro.com/sites/oakvillehydro/files/2024-07/Customer%20Choice%20Election%20Form_September%202023_0.pdf).
*   **Information Needed**: A recent electricity bill is required to provide your **electricity account number** and the **name your account is under**.

### 3. Effective Date of Change
*   **Timing**: A switch to Tiered prices can only take effect at the **start of a service period**.
*   **Service Period**: Accounts are generally billed for a **30-day period**. The start and end dates are identified on your electricity bill.
*   **Determining Next Service Period**: Look at the \"Present\" date (e.g., *11/03/2020 for meters starting with \"E\"*) on your last bill to find the start of the next service period.
*   **Submission Deadline**: The election form must be submitted **at least five business days** before the end of your current service period.
    *   *Example*: If your service period begins on November 3, 2020, and ends on December 3, 2020, you would need to submit the election form by November 26, 2020, for the change to be effective on your next bill.
*   **Late Submissions**: If the form is not received in time for the next bill, the change will take effect for the bill after that.

### 4. Acknowledgement and Processing
*   **Contact**: Oakville Hydro will contact you within **10 business days** of receiving your election form.
*   **Reasons for Delay/Rejection**: You will be informed if your form cannot be processed (*for instance, if youre not authorized to make changes to the account, or the account cant be verified*).
*   **Confirmation**: If approved, you will be notified of the expected date for the switch to your new pricing plan.
