## An easy guide to saving energy:

### **LOOK FOR THE BLUE STAR**

-   **SAVE TIME**: The symbol is a short-cut to find top energy performers
-   **SAVE ENERGY**: Certified products are in the top **15-30%** in their class for energy performance
-   **SAVE MONEY**: Using certified products cuts utility bills
-   **PROTECT THE PLANET**: Using certified products lowers greenhouse gas emissions  the GHGs saved from using ENERGY STAR products was equal to taking 100,000 cars off the road this year!
-   **YOU HAVE THE POWER**: Choose from over 80 types of ENERGY STAR certified products for home and work

### **WHATS BEHIND THE SYMBOL?**

-   **HIGH QUALITY**: They perform the same or better than standard products but are designed to save energy and money
-   **TESTED & CERTIFIED**: They are tested to meet or exceed strict technical specs. Energy performance is tested by a third-party
-   **BACKED BY THE GOVERNMENT OF CANADA**
