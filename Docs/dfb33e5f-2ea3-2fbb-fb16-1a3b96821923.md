# Importer's reminder card

## Importing regulated energy-using products

*Canadas Energy Efficiency Act and Energy Efficiency Regulations* prohibit the importation of certain energy-using products (for the purpose of sale or lease) unless specific requirements are met by importers/dealers.

## Importing requirements

_Our import reporting requirements (five data elements) are not required for battery chargers, external power supplies, fluorescent lamp ballasts, electric motors and small electric motors incorporated into another product at time of import._

1.  **Name of the product** (example: dishwasher)
2.  **Model number of the product** (for motors, the model number or the unique motor identifier)
3.  **Brand name** (if any)
4.  **Address of the dealer**
5.  **Purpose of the importation**
