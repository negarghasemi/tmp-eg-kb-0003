# Clothes dryers - Energy Efficiency Information

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory definition

**Clothes dryer**: A household tumble-type clothes dryer that is electrically operated.

### Compliance date

*   **Manufactured on or after**: January 1, 2015
*   *For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations.*

### Compliant product models

*   [Clothes dryers](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=CLOTHESDRYERS) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Labelling requirements

*   **EnerGuide label required**

### Testing standard

*   **CAN/CSA-C361-16** or Appendix D2 to Subpart B, Part 430 of Title 10 to the United States *Code of Federal Regulations*, entitled *Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Clothes Dryers*.

### Energy efficiency standard

**Energy performance standard for clothes dryers**

| Product type                          | Minimum combined energy factor (kg/kWh) | Minimum combined energy factor (lb/kWh) |
| :------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| Conventional standard electric        | **1.69**                                | **3.73**                                |
| Conventional compact electric 120 V   | **1.64**                                | **3.61**                                |
| Conventional compact electric 240 V   | **1.48**                                | **3.27**                                |
| Ventless compact electric 240 V       | **1.16**                                | **2.55**                                |
| Ventless combination washer-dryer electric | **0.94**                                | **2.08**                                |

*Notes: V = volts, kg/kWh = kilograms per kilowatt-hours, lb/kWh = pounds per kilowatt-hours*

### Energy efficiency report requirements

Reports must include the following information:

*   **Name of product**
*   **Brand name**
*   **Model number**
*   **Manufacturer**
*   The name of the certification body or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
*   Whether a mathematical model as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
*   **Annual energy consumption in kWh**
*   **Size category**: compact or standard
*   **Nominal voltage**
*   **Type of drying control**: timed, auto-temperature or auto-moisture
*   **Capacity**, in L, of clothes dryers drum
*   **Combined energy factor in kg/kWh**
