# Customer Choice - Q & A

## What is Customer Choice?
If youre a **residential or small business electricity customer**, you can choose to switch between **Time-of-Use (TOU)**, **Ultra-Low Overnight (ULO)** and **Tiered prices**.

## What do electricity plan rates pay for?
*   The **Ontario Energy Board (OEB)** sets electricity plan prices based on a forecast of the cost to supply Ontario customers with electricity over the next 12 months.
*   The OEB sets prices to recover the same forecast average cost of supply.
*   The **Regulated Price Plan (RPP)** aims to provide stable pricing, encourage conservation, and ensure prices reflect generation costs.
*   **Electricity utilities are not allowed to make a profit** from the sale of electricity.

## Will I save money by opting out of a price plan?
*   There is **no guarantee you will save money**, regardless of the electricity price plan you select.
*   The total bill impact depends on **how much electricity you consume** in a month and **when it is consumed** during the day.
*   For more information and to compare bill impacts, visit **[www.oeb.ca/choice](http://www.oeb.ca/choice)** and use the OEB bill calculator.

## What do I need to do to switch price plans?
*   **No action is required** if you wish to remain on your current price plan.
*   To switch, you need to:
    *   **Fill out an election form** (available on the Oakville Hydro website or by request).
    *   **Email the completed form to** `customerservice@oakvillehydro.com`.
    *   Alternatively, log into **MyOakvilleHydro**, click Choose your Electricity Price Plan', and then 'Switch Price Plan Now'.
    *   Have a recent electricity bill handy for your utility account number.
*   **Processing Time:**
    *   Oakville Hydro will notify you within **10 business days** if the form cannot be processed and why.
    *   If there are no issues, Oakville Hydro will inform you when the new price plan will take effect within the same 10 business days.
*   **Effective Date of Switch:**
    *   A switch can only take effect at the **start of a billing period** (generally about 30 days).
    *   If the form is received **at least 10 business days** before your next billing period starts, the switch will occur for that next billing period.
    *   If the form is received **less than 10 business days** before the start of your next billing period, the switch might occur for that period or the subsequent one.
    *   *Expect some time between submission and the actual change on your bill.* 

## If I have opted into Tiered prices, can I switch back to TOU prices or switch to ULO prices?
*   **Yes**, you can switch back.

## Can all customers opt out of TOU prices?
*   Any customer currently on TOU prices may opt out and pay Tiered prices instead.
*   If you were paying Tiered prices prior to November 1, 2024, your meter likely cannot bill TOU prices, and you cannot switch to TOU prices at this time. Contact Oakville Hydro for questions.
*   **Exclusions:** Customers of **unit sub-meter providers (USMPs)** (e.g., in condos or apartments where the bill comes from a company other than the electricity utility) cannot switch to Tiered prices. This decision rests with the **master consumer** (property manager, landlord, or condominium board).

## Will I lose my Ontario Electricity Support Program (OESP) credit if I switch to Tiered prices?
*   **No**, you will still receive the credit.

## Will I lose my Ontario Electricity Rebate if I switch to Tiered prices?
*   **No**, you will still receive the rebate.

## Will a new electricity utility inform me about price plan choices?
*   **Yes**, all electricity utilities in Ontario are required to inform new Regulated Price Plan customers about their choice between TOU, ULO, or Tiered prices when setting up an account.
