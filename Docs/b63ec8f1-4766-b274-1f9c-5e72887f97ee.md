# Solar Ready Guidelines

These guidelines, published by Natural Resources Canada, specify design considerations and modifications for new attached and detached homes to prepare them for future solar energy installations.

## Overview

*Solar Ready Guidelines* provide builders with recommendations for preparing new homes for the installation of either a solar thermal system or a solar photovoltaic system.

## Key Information

### Solar Ready Truss Design Procedures

*The Truss Plate Institute of Canadas Solar Ready Truss design* is highlighted as an option for truss fabricators. This design enables them to provide builders with trusses that can accommodate the anticipated structural loads associated with common solar thermal and solar photovoltaic systems.
