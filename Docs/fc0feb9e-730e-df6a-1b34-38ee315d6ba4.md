# Integrated Clothes Washer-Dryers

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition

*   **Integrated clothes washer-dryer:** A household appliance that consists of a clothes washer component and a clothes dryer component located above, below or beside it, that is powered by one power source and that has its control panel located on one of the components, or a combination clothes washer-dryer.
*   **Combination clothes washer-dryer:** A household appliance that has:
    *   a clothes washer function and clothes dryer function that utilize the same drum
    *   a common control panel for both functions, and
    *   one power source

### Compliance Date

*   **Clothes washer function:** Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2018**
*   **Clothes dryer function:** Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2015**

_For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations._

### Compliant Product Models

*   [Integrated clothes washers-dryers](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=ClothesWasher-Dryers) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Labelling Requirements

*   **EnerGuide label required**

### Testing Standard

*   **Clothes washer function:** CSA C360-13
*   **Clothes dryer function:** CSA C361-16 or Appendix D2 to Subpart B, Part 430 of Title 10 to the United States _Code of Federal Regulations_, entitled _Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Clothes Dryers_

#### Energy Performance Standard for Integrated Clothes Washer-Dryers

**Clothes Washer Function**

| Product Type                 | Integrated Modified Energy Factor (L/kWh/cycle) | Integrated Water Factor (L/cycle/L) |
| :--------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------- |
| Vertical-axis compact        |  32.56                                         |  1.60                              |
| Vertical-axis standard       |  44.46                                         |  0.87                              |
| Horizontal-axis compact      |  32.00                                         |  1.11                              |
| Horizontal-axis standard     |  52.10                                         |  0.63                              |

**Clothes Dryer Function**

| Product Class                            | Minimum Combined Energy Factor (kg/kWh) | Minimum Combined Energy Factor (lb/kWh) |
| :--------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| Conventional standard electric           | 1.69                                    | 3.73                                    |
| Conventional compact electric 120 V      | 1.64                                    | 3.61                                    |
| Conventional compact electric 240        | 1.48                                    | 3.27                                    |

| Ventless combination washer-dryer electric | 0.94                                    | 2.08                                    |

*   **L/kWh/cycle** = litres per kilowatt-hour per cycle
*   **L/cycle/L** = litres per cycle per litre
*   **kg/kWh** = kilograms per kilowatt-hour
*   **lb/kWh** = pounds per kilowatt-hour
*   **V** = volts

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements

*   Name of product
*   Brand name
*   Model number
*   Manufacturer
*   The name of the certification body or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
*   Whether a mathematical model as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
*   Type and size category
*   Volume, expressed in litres
*   Annual energy consumption in L/kWh/cycle for clothes washer function
*   Combined energy factor in kg/kWh for clothes dryer function
*   Integrated water consumption factor in L/cycle/L
*   Maximum water temperature available, namely,  57.2C or > 57.2C
*   Which of the wash/rinse temperature selections listed in Table 5 of CSA C360-13 the product has
*   Water fill control system the product has, namely, manual or adaptive
*   Drying controls the product has, namely, timed, auto-temp or auto-moisture
