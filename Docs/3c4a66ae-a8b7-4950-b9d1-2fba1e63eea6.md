# Energy Journey for Condo Dwellers

This report provides various tips and recommendations for condo dwellers to improve energy efficiency and save on energy costs.

## Energy-Saving Tips and Recommendations

### Lighting Efficiency: Watts, Kelvins, and Lumens

*   **Understanding LED Lighting**: When purchasing LED bulbs, focus on **lumens** for brightness and **kelvins** for color temperature, rather than watts, as LEDs consume less energy.
    *   **Lumens**: *Measure of a bulb's brightness; higher lumens mean a brighter bulb.*
    *   **Kelvins**: *Measure of color temperature, ranging from warm yellow to cool soft white.*
*   **Further Information**: [Learn more in our lighting guide](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Advice-and-Tips/Lighting-guide)

### Draft Proofing: Weather Stripping Wins

*   **Action**: Use **weather stripping** on windows and doors to seal cracks.
*   **Benefit**: This helps prevent cold drafts, leading to an airtight home that reduces energy costs and increases comfort.
*   **Video Guide**: A video explains the benefits of weather stripping.

### Natural Humidity Management: Plants as Humidifiers

*   **Tip**: Some indoor plants can naturally add humidity to the air.
*   **Recommended Plants**: Peace lilies, Boston ferns, English ivy, and desert-thriving succulents like aloe.
*   **Benefit**: Reduces the need for electric humidifiers during drier winter months.
*   **Further Information**: [Learn more about balancing humidity at home](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Advice-and-Tips/How-to-manage-humidity-at-home)

### Smart Heating and Cooling: Smart Thermostats

*   **Recommendation**: Install a **smart thermostat**.
*   **Function**: Acts as your homes temperature moderator, learning your preferences and optimizing heating/cooling.
*   **Benefit**: Helps save on energy use by efficiently managing your home's climate.
*   **Further Information**: [Learn how to maximize savings with your smart thermostat](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Advice-and-Tips/Smart-Thermostat-Savings)

### Efficient Gaming: Reducing Phantom Power

*   **Issue**: Gaming consoles consume energy even in sleep mode due to **phantom power**.
*   **Action**: Enable your console's energy-saving mode.
*   **Benefit**: Cuts down on unnecessary energy use.
*   **Guide**: [Learn how to configure gaming consoles to use less energy](https://www.energystar.gov/products/configuring_todays_game_consoles_use_less_energy_0)

### Advanced Lighting Control: Smart Lighting Beyond Bulbs

*   **Recommendation**: Consider **smart plugs and switches** in addition to smart bulbs.
*   **Benefit**: Provides a convenient way to manage home lighting and helps save energy.
*   **Further Information**: [Read our smart lighting buying guide](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Advice-and-Tips/Smart-lighting-buying-guide)
