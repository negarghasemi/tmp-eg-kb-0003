# General Service Lamp Labelling Design Templates

According to **NRCans Energy Efficiency Regulations**, general service lamps manufactured on or after **January 1st, 2027**, must follow the labelling requirements outlined in [Amendment 18](https://gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2025/2025-04-09/html/sor-dors110-eng.html).

## Covered Lamp Types
General service lamps include:
-   **Compact fluorescent lamps**
-   **General service incandescent lamps**
-   **General service incandescent reflector lamps**
-   **General service light-emitting diode (LED) lamps**
-   **General service organic light-emitting diode (OLED) lamps**

## Purpose of Label Design Templates
NRCan offers professionally developed label design templates for manufacturers. While the format is voluntary, it aligns with NRCan's labelling requirements for general service lamps. For more details on energy efficiency requirements, refer to the [general service lamp webpage](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/general-service-lamps-0).

The proposed design aims to:
-   Increase the labels visibility.
-   Ensure a common look for information provided.
-   Permit comparison among products using different technologies.
-   Encourage the selection of energy-efficient products, leading to energy and economic savings.

## Descriptions and Examples

### Template Details
-   Templates are available in multiple electronic formats: **.JPG**, **.PNG**, and **.EPS++**.
-   They are provided in the size they should appear on packaging.
-   They can be **enlarged** to accommodate larger packaging.
-   They can be **reduced up to 30%** to fit smaller packaging.
-   For **three-way lamps**, a specific template can be used to display information for each level of operation.

### Information about Graphic File Formats
-   **JPG:** *Common format for digital images, compatible with most software.*
-   **PNG:** *Format primarily used for logos and online graphics, compatible with most software.*
-   **EPS:** *High-quality format often used by professional graphic designers with specialized software (e.g., Adobe Illustrator).*

## Terms
Terms are defined in the Regulations or refer to metrics currently defined in the Regulations:
-   **Light output (Flux lumineux):** *Refers to luminous flux in lumens (lm).*
-   **Efficacy (Efficacit):** *Refers to the measured lumen output of a lamp divided by the lamp power (lm/W).*
-   **Power (Puissance):** *Refers to lamp power in watts (W).*
-   **Life (Dure de vie):** *Refers to lamp life in hours.*
-   **Light appearance (Apparence de la lumire):** *Refers to correlated colour temperature in degrees Kelvin (K).*
