# Check your eligibility for the Canada Greener Homes Grant

## Initial Ineligibility Conditions
If any of the following conditions apply, you are **ineligible** for this grant:

*   **I am a landlord and rent my property.**
*   **I am a renter.**
*   **I already started or completed my retrofits without first receiving an EnerGuide home evaluation.**
*   **My home was built less than 6 months ago** *(but you can apply once your home is at least 6 months old).* 
*   **I have not moved into the home yet because we are renovating it, before making it my primary residence.**

## Eligibility Scenarios and Outcomes

### Scenario 1: Homeowner with Primary Residence

#### Property Type

*   **Single or semi-detached house, mobile home on a permanent foundation, or permanently moored floating home:** Likely eligible, proceeds to next checks.
*   **Row house, townhome, or mixed-use building (residential portion only):** Checks if property is freehold.
*   **Home/building with a secondary suite or apartment, or a multi-unit residential building (MURB):** Checks MURB criteria.
*   **Housing cooperative:** Ineligible for individual applicants. *Exception: Indigenous government or organization applications.* (See Scenario 2)
*   **None of the above:** Ineligible. (See Outcome 3)

#### MURB Criteria (for secondary suites, apartments, or MURBs)

To be eligible, the following criteria must be met:

*   The entire building, not just individual units, will receive an EnerGuide evaluation.
*   The decision-makers of the building will approve eligible and recommended retrofits.
*   At least 50% of the building is residential living space and people live in it year-round.
*   The building will meet the conditions to receive a blower door test.

*   **If all MURB criteria are met:** Proceeds to next checks.
*   **If MURB criteria are NOT met:** Ineligible. (See Outcome 6)

#### Freehold Property Check (for row houses, townhomes, or mixed-use buildings)

*   **If property is freehold:** Proceeds to next checks.
*   **If property is NOT freehold:** May need approval from condo board. (See Warning 3)

#### EnerGuide Evaluation Status

*   **Already received a pre-retrofit EnerGuide evaluation:** Proceed with caution, as eligibility depends on the evaluation date. (See Warning 2)
*   **Not yet received a pre-retrofit EnerGuide evaluation:** Proceeds to next checks.

#### Retrofit Timeline

*   **1 to 6 months:** Warning about longer processing times. (See Warning 1)
*   **Sometime this year or no particular timeline:** Proceeds to next checks.
*   **Already started or completed retrofits:** Ineligible unless specific conditions are met. (See Outcome 9)

#### Most Important Retrofit (for those who have NOT started retrofits)

*   **Home insulation, air-sealing, windows and doors, space and water heating, renewable energy (e.g., solar panels), or resiliency measures (e.g., moisture-proofing or roofing membrane):** Good fit, potentially eligible. (See Outcome 4)
*   **Thermostat:** Only eligible if combined with at least one other major retrofit. (See Outcome 5)
*   **None of the above (ineligible types):** Ineligible. (See Outcome 7)

#### Retrofit Started/Completed (for those who HAVE started retrofits)

*   **Home insulation, air-sealing, windows and doors, space and water heating, renewable energy (e.g., solar panels), or resiliency measures (e.g., moisture-proofing or roofing membrane):** Ineligible if done before evaluation or specific dates. (See Outcome 9)
*   **Thermostat:** Ineligible if done before evaluation or specific dates. (See Outcome 8)
*   **None of the above (ineligible types):** Ineligible if done before evaluation or specific dates. (See Outcome 9)

### Scenario 2: Indigenous Government or Organization

*   **If you represent an Indigenous government or organization:** You are eligible under specific conditions. (See Outcome 2)

### Scenario 3: None of the Above

*   **If none of the above descriptions apply to you:** You are not eligible. (See Outcome 1)

## Outcomes and Warnings

### Outcome 1: Not Eligible (General)

*   **Reason:** The Canada Greener Homes Grant is intended for homeowners whose property is their primary residence. Landlords and renters are not eligible. Your primary residence must be one of the eligible property types.
*   **Recommendation:** Explore other financial incentives in your region for energy upgrades.

### Outcome 2: Indigenous Government or Organization Eligibility

*   **Eligibility:** Eligible to register multiple homes. Homes do not need to be the owners primary residence but must be occupied by an Indigenous household.
*   **Off-grid communities:** Indigenous groups can check the Remote Energy Communities Database.
*   **Application:** Call 1-833-674-8282 or apply online through the homeowner portal for Indigenous homeowners living off-reserve or with alternative proof of ownership.

### Outcome 3: Not Eligible (Property Type)

*   **Reason:** Your primary residence must be one of the eligible property types.
*   **Recommendation:** Explore other financial incentives in your region for energy upgrades.

### Outcome 4: Good Fit!

*   **Grant Details:** Up to $600 for two EnerGuide evaluations and up to $5,000 for retrofits. The full amount is not guaranteed.
*   **Condition:** You must complete both evaluations and at least one recommended retrofit to receive the grant.
*   **Before you apply:** Understand the process, have a valid government-issued ID with your primary address, have your tax roll number, and find service organizations for evaluations.
*   **Provincial Conditions:** Specific conditions apply to Nova Scotia, Ontario, Quebec, New Brunswick, Rossland (BC), Nelson and Regional District of Central Kootenay (BC).
*   **Planning:** Allow more time due to the multi-step process and high interest.
*   **Canada Greener Homes Loan:** Interest-free loan up to $40,000 for expensive retrofits, with a repayment period of up to 10 years. Must be approved for the grant first.
*   **Next Steps:** Learn about the grant process, eligible retrofits, and start your application.

### Outcome 5: Grant Might Not Be Right (Thermostat Only)

*   **Reason:** The grant provides only up to $50 for thermostats and only if you are also **completing at least one other retrofit** from your pre-retrofit EnerGuide evaluation. Thermostats must be combined with another eligible retrofit.
*   **Recommendation:** Learn more about eligible products and installations before applying.

### Outcome 6: Not Eligible (MURB Criteria)

*   **Reason:** You did not meet all the additional criteria for multi-unit residential buildings.
*   **Recommendation:** Explore other financial incentives in your region for energy upgrades.

### Outcome 7: Grant Might Not Be Right (Ineligible Retrofits)

*   **Reason:** The Canada Greener Homes Grant supports specific energy-efficient and resilient retrofits. Many types of upgrades are ineligible.
*   **Ineligible Upgrades Include:**
    *   Retrofits done before a pre-retrofit evaluation.
    *   Retrofits done before December 1, 2020.
    *   Eligible products installed in new home additions built between pre- and post-retrofit evaluations.
    *   Leased or rented equipment.
    *   Air conditioners.
    *   Tankless or storage water heaters other than heat pump water heaters.
    *   Skylights.
    *   Furnaces (unless in northern or off-grid community).
    *   Boilers (unless in northern or off-grid community).
    *   Roofs.
*   **Recommendation:** Learn more about eligible products and installations.

### Outcome 8: Grant Might Not Be Right (Thermostat - Already Started)

*   **Reason:** The grant provides only up to $50 for thermostats and only if you are also **completing at least one other retrofit** from your pre-retrofit EnerGuide evaluation. Thermostats must be combined with another eligible retrofit. Additionally, retrofits done before a pre-retrofit evaluation or before December 1, 2020, are generally ineligible.
*   **Recommendation:** Learn more about eligible products and installations.

### Outcome 9: Grant Might Not Be Right (Major Retrofits - Already Started)

*   **Reason:** The Canada Greener Homes Grant supports specific energy-efficient and resilient retrofits. Retrofits done before a pre-retrofit evaluation or before December 1, 2020, are generally ineligible.
*   **Recommendation:** Learn more about eligible products and installations.

### Warning 1: Allow More Time in Your Planning

*   **Note:** The grant process has several steps (application review, eligibility confirmation, pre/post-retrofit EnerGuide evaluations, payment processing) and can take time. Timelines may vary due to high interest.

### Warning 2: EnerGuide Evaluation Timing

*   **Evaluation before April 1, 2020:** You need a new evaluation.
*   **Evaluation between April 1, 2020, and November 30, 2020:** Can use it, but no grant for the evaluation itself.
*   **Evaluation between December 1, 2020, and June 30, 2021:** Eligible for evaluation and retrofit grants if at least one eligible retrofit is completed on or after December 1, 2020 (retrofit doesn't need to be recommended).
*   **Evaluation on or after July 1, 2021:** Eligible for evaluation and retrofit grants if you complete both evaluations and at least one recommended retrofit.

### Warning 3: Condo Board Approval

*   **Note:** Contact your condo or strata board if you plan any exterior retrofits.
