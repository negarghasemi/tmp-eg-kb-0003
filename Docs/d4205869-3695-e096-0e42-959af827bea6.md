# Ventilating Fans

An ENERGY STAR certified ventilating fan or exhaust fan (also known as a ventilation or vent fan) with lighting **uses 70% less energy**, on average, than a standard model. This includes bathroom/utility room fans and range hoods.

Ventilating fans that have earned the *ENERGY STAR Most Efficient* designation are the best of the best  theyll save you more energy than standard models. Saving energy saves money and reduces your carbon footprint.

## Key Features
- Use high performance motors and improved blade designs, meaning theyre more efficient, less noisy, and last longer.

## The ENERGY STAR Promise
All ENERGY STAR certified products are tested to meet strict [efficiency specifications](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/technical-specifications) and are certified by an independent third party. They perform the same as or better than standard products without compromising performance in any way.

Start your research using the [ENERGY STAR Product Finder tool to find and compare](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-ventilating-fans/results?SetLanguage=English&NRCAN=on) certified ventilating fans (*You will be redirected to the U.S. ENERGY STAR website*).

## Helpful Tips
- Use a ventilating fan together with an ENERGY STAR certified [dehumidifier](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-star/products/list-certified-products/dehumidifiers) to remove moist air locally.

## Regulations
Ventilating fans are subject to Canada's *Energy Efficiency Regulations*, which set a minimum performance standard for their energy efficiency. Find details in the [Guide to the Regulations](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/guide-canada-s-energy-efficiency-regulations).
