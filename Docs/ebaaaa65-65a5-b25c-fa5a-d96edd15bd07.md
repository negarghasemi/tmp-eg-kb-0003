# Home Efficiency Rebate Plus

## Program Update: Home Efficiency Rebate Plus

![Enbridge Gas and the Government of Canada Canada Greener Homes Grant](https://www.enbridgegas.com/-/media/Extranet-Pages/ontario/residential/rebates-energy-conservation/Home-Efficiency-Rebate-Plus/logos-horizontal-pause.jpg?h=80&w=796&rev=b12f374db5f449238a11e9b56fba4944&extension=webp&hash=AE2B3946970C8DFCE4E164D932815D3D)

### The new Home Renovation Savings Program

Enbridge Gas and Save on Energy, with support from the Ontario Government, have partnered to launch the new Home Renovation Savings program to help Ontarians improve home energy efficiency and comfort.* Homeowners now have more choice, with flexible options to choose single or bundled upgrades for greater savings.

[Find out more](https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/)

The Home Efficiency Rebate Plus program, delivered in partnership with the Canada Greener Homes Grant, has been a huge success. We have helped over 100,000 Ontario households reduce their energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions.

To ensure that Enbridge Gas and Canada Greener Homes Grant can effectively manage the remaining grant funding available within the program, we have established **a deadline of December 1, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. (ET) for all post-retrofit energy assessments to be completed. This gives your Registered Energy Advisor a couple of weeks to submit all paperwork, as all applications must be submitted by December 31, 2025.** Requests for grant payments made after the deadline will not be processed and no exceptions can be made. Reminder that on Feb. 5, 2024, we closed intake of new applicants to the HER+ program, and all pre-retrofit energy assessments must have been completed by that date.

This notice is to ensure homeowners have time, to complete their application and have it submitted.

**Please note**: As a result of this update, companies unaffiliated with Enbridge Gas or Canadas Greener Homes Grant may take advantage and promote misinformation through various channels such as (but not limited to) social media, print ads, email, phone, tv and radio ads. Please visit the [Enbridge Gas consumer protection page](/ontario/contact-us/scam-awareness) for tips to protect yourself from a potential scam. You can also visit our FAQs below for a [list of Service Organizations](#faq) certified by Natural Resources Canada (NRCan).

## Frequently asked questions

-   **When is the program ending?**
    To qualify for the CGHG funding, all post-retrofit energy assessments must be completed by Dec. 1, 2025. This gives your Registered Energy Advisor time to submit all paperwork, so your file is fully submitted by the Dec. 31, 2025 deadline.

-   **Why am I still seeing ads promoting rebates for home upgrades?**
    Please check the Enbridge Gas [Rebates & Energy Conservation](/ontario/rebates-energy-conservation) section to learn about other energy saving programs such as [Home Renovation Savings](https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/without-assessment/heat-pumps). As a result of the end of the program, companies unaffiliated with Enbridge Gas or the Canada Greener Homes Grant may take advantage and promote misinformation through various channels such as (but not limited to) social media, print ads, email, phone, TV and radio ads. Please visit the [Enbridge Gas consumer protection page](/ontario/contact-us/scam-awareness) for tips to protect yourself from potential scams. You can also read our FAQs below for a list of service organizations certified by Natural Resources Canada (NRCan).

-   **Why is HER+ ending the program early ?**
    To ensure that Enbridge Gas and Canada Greener Homes Grant can effectively manage the remaining grant funding available within the program. we have established a deadline of Dec. 1, 2025, for all post-retrofit energy assessments to be completed. This gives the Registered Energy Advisors until Dec. 31, 2025 to submit all paperwork required. Requests for grant payments made after the deadline will not be processed and no exceptions can be made.
    This notice is to ensure homeowners have at least four months' notice, to complete their post-retrofit energy assessment.

-   **Who do I contact if I have a question about my application?**
    Please reach out to your Registered Energy Advisor for any questions regarding assessments or what rebates you qualify for, or if you feel like something was missed in your application.

-   **I have already completed my pre and post-retrofit energy assessment, does this update impact me?**
    If your pre and post-retrofit energy assessment have both been completed, you should receive your rebate as expected. If you completed your post-retrofit energy assessment more than three months ago and have not received any communication, please reach out to your service organization to ensure nothing was missed.

-   **What if my post-retrofit energy assessment is scheduled after Dec 1, 2025?**
    HER+ applications will only be accepted if the pre-retrofit energy assessments completed between April 1, 2020, and Feb. 5, 2024, and the post-retrofit energy assessment was completed between Jan. 1, 2023, and Dec. 1, 2025. Requests for grant payments made after the deadline will not be processed and no exceptions can be made. Reminder that although the final date to submit HER+ applications by your Registered Energy Advisor is Dec 31, 2025, it can take a few weeks for them to submit all documentation which is why the final date to complete a post-retrofit energy assessment is Dec 1, 2025. We recommend you book your post-retrofit energy assessment as soon as possible so your Registered Energy Advisor has lots of time to submit your documentation.
    As a result of the intake to the Home Efficiency Rebate Plus program ending, companies unaffiliated with Enbridge Gas or Canadas Greener Homes Grant may take advantage and promote misinformation through various channels such as (but not limited to) social media, print ads, email, phone, TV and radio ads. Please visit the Enbridge Gas [Consumer Protection page](/ontario/contact-us/scam-awareness) for tips to protect yourself from a potential scam.

-   **How long before I receive my rebate?**
    You should receive your rebate cheque within 16 weeks after your Registered Energy Advisor submits your application. We are working to process these applications as quickly as possible. Make sure you have provided your Registered Energy Advisor with the best address to send your cheque to, so it arrives as soon as possible. We also ask for your email to provide you with updates along the way.

-   **Who do I contact if there are problem with the retrofit contractor I used for the HER+ program?**
    If you encounter any issues or concerns regarding any of the authorized retrofits, upgrades or improvements implemented as part of the Home Efficiency Rebate Plus (HER+) program, or any issues or concerns regarding the contractor you have hired to implement any such authorized retrofits, upgrades or improvements, you must address such issues or concerns directly with the contractor. Enbridge Gas is not affiliated with any such contractor, and is in no way responsible for referring, selecting, or directly contracting with any such contractors.

-   **I received a letter saying my file has been opted in or out of CGHG/ HER+, is this a scam?**
    Enbridge Gas and the Canada Greener Homes Grant are cleaning up their databases to ensure as many people can be included in the program as possible. Please reach out to your registered energy advisor or call the Enbridge Energy Conservation Line at 1-844-777-3149 if you believe you have been inaccurately opt out.
