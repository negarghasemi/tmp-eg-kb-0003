# Check your eligibility for the Oil to Heat Pump Affordability program

## Start here

*Recommended:* Right click on the links and click open link in a new tab to avoid restarting this form.

If any of the following conditions applies, **you are ineligible** for this grant:

*   I am a landlord and rent my property.
*   I am a renter.
*   My home is not connected to an integrated electricity grid (for example, the North American electricity grid).
*   My home was built less than 6 months ago. (_You can apply when your home is at least 6 months old_).

## Getting more information

Provide an answer to reveal the next question.

### Tell us a bit about you:

**Question 1:** I live in British Columbia, New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, Nova Scotia, Ontario, Prince Edward Island or Yukon?

*   **If Yes:** You are directed to visit your provincial or territorial program website to verify eligibility and participate. Links are provided for:
    *   [British Columbia](https://www.betterhomesbc.ca/energy-savings-program/)
    *   [New Brunswick](https://www.saveenergynb.ca/en/for-home/oil-to-heat-pump-affordability/)
    *   [Newfoundland and Labrador](https://takechargenl.ca/oiltoelectric/)
    *   [Nova Scotia](https://www.efficiencyns.ca/programs-rebates/oil-to-heat-pump-affordability-program)
    *   [Ontario](https://saveonenergy.ca/en/For-Your-Home/Energy-Affordability-Program)
    *   [Prince Edward Island](https://www.princeedwardisland.ca/en/service/free-heat-pump-program)
    *   [Yukon](https://yukon.ca/en/heating-system-rebate#affordable-heat-pump-program-)

*   **If No:** Proceed to Question 2.

**Question 2:** I live in Nunavut, in a remote area of Quebec, or in another remote area in Canada.

*   **If Yes:** You are ineligible for the program. Your home must be connected to an integrated electricity grid. Homeowners in Nunavut are encouraged to explore alternative financial programs.
    *   [Financial incentives by province/territory](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/financial-incentive-province).

*   **If No:** Proceed to Question 3.

**Question 3:** I am a Canadian homeowner with proof that my home is my primary residence.

*   **If Yes:** Proceed to Question 4.

*   **If No:** You are ineligible for the program. The program is for homeowners whose property is their current and primary residence.
    *   [Explore other financial incentives to help you make changes at home](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/financial-incentive-province).

**Question 5:** I currently heat my home with oil\* (*You purchased at least 500 litres of oil in the past 12 months and have retained your receipts*).

*   **If Yes:** Proceed to Question 6.

*   **If No:** You are ineligible for the program. This program is for homeowners whose primary heat source is oil (natural gas, propane, or coal are *not* included). You need to provide receipts for 500 litres of heating oil purchased in the past 12 months.
    *   [Explore other financial incentives to help you make changes at home](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/financial-incentive-province).

**Question 6:** My home is a single home, a duplex, or a single home with a secondary suite, such as a basement apartment.

*   *Your primary residence must be one of the eligible property types to qualify for this program:*
    *   Single or semi-detached house
    *   Row house or townhome
    *   Mobile home on a permanent foundation
    *   Permanently moored floating home
    *   Low-rise multi-unit residential buildings (two units only)
    *   Mixed-use building (residential portion only)
    *   Learn more about [eligible property types](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/canada-greener-homes-initiative/eligible-property-types).

*   ***Not eligible:*** Multi-unit residential buildings (MURBs) with 3 or more units regardless of size are ineligible.

*   **If Yes:** Based on what you told us, you may be a good fit!
    *   Your selections in this eligibility form have determined that you seem to be eligible for the Oil to Heat Pump Affordability program. There will be additional eligibility criteria and verifications assessed as you proceed.
    *   [Find out how the process works](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/canada-greener-homes-initiative/process-works).

*   **If No:** You are ineligible for the program. Your primary residence must be one of the [eligible property types](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/canada-greener-homes-initiative/eligible-property-types) to qualify.
    *   [Explore other financial incentives to help you make changes at home](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/home-energy-efficiency/financial-incentive-province).
