# Property Energy Efficiency Report

## General Energy Saving Tips

This report outlines simple ways to improve energy efficiency and keep your home warm and cozy, focusing on general home improvements and appliance usage.

### Keeping Your Home Warm and Energy-Efficient

#### Recommendations:

*   **Applying weather stripping**: Apply weather stripping to windows and doors to reduce unwanted drafts.
*   **Utilize natural light**: Open blinds and curtains on sunny days to warm rooms naturally.

#### Benefits:

*   **Reduced heat loss**: Weather stripping helps prevent heat from escaping.
*   **Lower energy bills**: Minimizing drafts and using natural heat can decrease heating costs.
*   **Natural warming**: Sunshine brightens and warms your home throughout the day.

#### Go Further:

*   **Install energy-efficient windows**: This can significantly reduce energy costs and maintain comfortable indoor temperatures year-round.

### Efficient Dishwasher Usage

#### Key Facts & Comparisons:

*   **Water Consumption**: Most energy-efficient dishwashers use **fewer than 15 litres of water** for a full cycle, whereas handwashing typically uses about **15 litres every two minutes**.
*   **ENERGY STAR Dishwashers**: These models can save an average of **26,400 litres of water annually**, equivalent to almost 87 standard bathtubs.

#### Recommendations:

*   **Load your dishwasher fully**: Dishwashers are more energy and water-efficient when run with a full load, including plates, bowls, cutlery, pots, and pans.
*   **Partial loads are still efficient**: Even running a dishwasher with less than a full load can save energy and water compared to handwashing.

#### Go Further:

*   **Optimize dishwasher settings**: Use settings appropriate for the dishes' dirtiness, as these adjust water and heat usage.
*   **Utilize no-heat air drying**: Choose this option to save additional energy by avoiding the heating element for drying.
