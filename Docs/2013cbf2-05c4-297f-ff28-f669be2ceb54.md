# Smart thermostats - infographic

This infographic provides an overview of ENERGY STAR certified smart thermostats, highlighting their features, certification benefits, and potential savings.

## Overview
Smart thermostats that earn the ENERGY STAR label are independently certified to deliver reliable performance and energy savings.

## Key Features

-   **Learning Capabilities**: Learn your temperature preferences and establish a schedule that adjusts to energy-saving temperatures when youre asleep or away.
-   **Energy Data**: Provide home energy use data that you can track and manage.
-   **Remote Control**: Give you control of home heating and cooling remotely through your smartphone.

## Certification Standards
ENERGY STAR smart thermostats are third-party certified to:

-   **Data Tracking**: Track and report equipment use and temperature data to the homeowner.
-   **Low-Power Standby**: Quickly enter a low-power standby mode when inactive.
-   **Proven Energy Savings**: Save energy based on field data collected from over one thousand homes over an entire year.

## Potential Savings (If widely adopted)
If everyone used an ENERGY STAR certified smart thermostat, the projected annual savings would be:

-   **Energy**: **11 petajoules** (equivalent to energy used in about 700,000 electrical vehicles per year)
-   **Cost**: **$248 million**
-   **Greenhouse Gas Emissions**: Offsetting **774 kilotonnes** (equivalent to 1.5 of the greenhouse gas emissions by Nunavut in 2015)

## Additional Savings Opportunities

-   **Utility Rebates**: Utilities or efficiency programs in your area may offer rebates on ENERGY STAR certified smart thermostats.
-   **Financial Rewards**: In some areas, homeowners with smart thermostats can participate in utility programs that support reliable power for everyone, and earn financial rewards for it.

## Benefits of ENERGY STAR Certified Smart Thermostats

-   **Demonstrated Energy Savings**
-   **Reliable Performance**
-   **Environmental Benefits**
-   **Convenience, Insight, and Control**
