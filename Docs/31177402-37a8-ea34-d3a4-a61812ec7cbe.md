# Drain Water Heat Recovery

## Recycling Heated Waste Water

Dont let your energy dollars go down the drain.

A **drain water heat recovery (DWHR) pipe** is a simple technology designed to **save on energy used to heat water**. This system utilizes warm water flowing down drains to preheat the water entering the hot water tank, thereby reducing the energy required to reach the desired temperature.

### How it Works

*   **Cold water** entering your home passes through a series of coils tightly wrapped around your drain stack.
*   **Heat from the warm drain water** is transferred through the copper walls of the heat recovery unit to warm the cold water.
*   The preheated water then flows into the water heater.
*   **Important**: The drain water and fresh water never mix.

### Key Features and Benefits

*   **Simple Technology**: No moving parts.
*   **Durability**: Long-lasting system.
*   **Effectiveness**: DWHR units are most effective during large drain water events (e.g., showers) where significant warm water is drained while simultaneously drawing cold potable water.
