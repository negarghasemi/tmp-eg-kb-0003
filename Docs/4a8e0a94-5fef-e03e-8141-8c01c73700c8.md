# Electric Heat Pumps

## The Cool Truth About Electric Heat Pumps

Electric heat pumps are energy-efficient heating and cooling systems that provide comfortable temperatures for your home year-round. More Canadians are switching to this technology for their homes and businesses to save energy and reduce their carbon footprint.

### Heat Pump Statistics and Goals

| Metric                                                                           | Value        |
| :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------- |
| Canadian homes using electric heat pumps (as of 2023)                            | **7%**       |
| Canadian homes still using fossil fuel heating                                   | **54%**      |
| Canadian homeowners planning to replace primary heating systems in next 5 years  | **1 in 4**   |
| Residential greenhouse gas emissions from space and water heating                | **99%**      |
| Heat pumps shipped to Canada in 2021 (20% more than gas furnaces)                | **300,000**  |

*To reach our goal of net-zero emissions by **2050 in the residential sector**, we must transform space and water heating in buildings.*
