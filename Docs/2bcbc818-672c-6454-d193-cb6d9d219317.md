# Electric Heaters: Energy Efficiency Overview

## Common Energy Users Across Canada
Electric space heaters, including electric furnaces, are widely used in Canadian homes and businesses, serving as either primary or supplementary heat sources.

### Technologies and Features
*   **Types**: Fan-forced, convection, and radiant.
*   **Installation**: Available as portable or installed units.
*   **Control**: Thermostats can be used to manage temperature and, consequently, energy consumption.

### Energy Efficiency Considerations
*   **Technical Efficiency**: Electric space heaters achieve 100% energy efficiency at the point of use, meaning they produce one unit of heat for every unit of electricity consumed.
*   **Comparative Efficiency**: Despite their technical efficiency, they are not always the most efficient heating choice. For instance, geothermal heat pumps can deliver up to three units of heat for every unit of electricity used, translating to 300% energy efficiency.

### Regulations and Certifications
*   **Energy Efficiency Regulations (Canada)**: Electric space heaters are generally *not* covered by Canada's `Energy Efficiency Regulations`, with the sole exception of a technology requirement for electric boilers.
*   **ENERGY STAR Qualification**: These products are currently *not* available in ENERGY STAR qualified high-efficiency models.
