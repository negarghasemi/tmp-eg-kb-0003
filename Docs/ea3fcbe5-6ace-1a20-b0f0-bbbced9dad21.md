# Eligible products for my home retrofit

## Program Status
*   **Canada Greener Homes Grant:** Closed. December 31, 2025, was the last day to submit documents. Requests submitted by the deadline will continue to be processed.
*   **Canada Greener Homes Loan:** Applications closed. October 1, 2025, was the last day to apply.

## Overview of Eligible Products
Some retrofits under the Canada Greener Homes Grant required choosing an eligible product from an approved list. The chosen equipment must have been selected from the applicable eligible product list, which included:

-   windows and doors
-   ground source heat pumps
-   air source heat pumps
-   cold climate air source heat pumps
-   heat pump water heaters
-   gas-fired boilers (for northern and off-grid communities only)
-   gas-fired furnaces (for northern and off-grid communities only)
-   oil-fired boilers (for northern and off-grid communities only)
-   oil-fired furnaces (for northern and off-grid communities only)

*It was recommended to learn about product and equipment eligibility criteria and consult the list of eligible products before starting upgrades.*

**Important:** All products must be purchased in Canada. Online purchases are eligible only if they were ordered from a distributor located in Canada.

*Note: Regular quarterly updates to the Canada Greener Homes Grant searchable product list occurred in **March, June, September, and December of each year** to ensure homeowners had access to grants for all products available that meet the performance criteria.*

## Windows and Doors
To receive a grant, homeowners had to purchase windows and doors that were **ENERGY STAR certified** and appeared on the approved lists. ENERGY STAR certified models meet or exceed certification levels.

*Do not remove ENERGY STAR labels until after your post-retrofit evaluation, as these labels help your energy advisor confirm product certification.*

**Eligible ENERGY STAR Certified Windows and Doors (Grant Amount: $125 per rough opening):**
-   [ENERGY STAR certified windows](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=WINDOWS_GH)
-   [ENERGY STAR certified sliding glass doors](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=DOORS_SGD_GH)
-   [ENERGY STAR certified hinged doors, sidelites and transoms](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=DOORS_GH)

**Eligible ENERGY STAR MOST EFFICIENT Windows and Doors (Grant Amount: $250 per rough opening):**
-   [ENERGY STAR MOST EFFICIENT windows](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=WINDOWS_ME_GH)
-   [ENERGY STAR MOST EFFICIENT sliding glass doors](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=DOORS_SGD_ME_GH)

## Mechanical Systems
Mechanical systems include:
-   ground source heat pumps
-   air source heat pumps
-   cold climate air source heat pumps
-   heat pump water heaters
-   furnaces (*eligible for northern and off-grid community residents only*)
-   boilers (*eligible for northern and off-grid community residents only*)

## Canada Greener Homes Initiative: Additional Programs

-   **Oil to Heat Pump Affordability program:** Helps low- to median-income homeowners switch from oil heating to an eligible electric heat pump system. *Details vary by province and territory.*
-   **Canada Greener Homes Affordability Program:** Helps low- to median-income homeowners and tenants reduce energy bills and greenhouse gas emissions through no-cost home retrofits, delivered through provincial and territorial partners, with additional support for Indigenous organizations.
