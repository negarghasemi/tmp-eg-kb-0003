# Oil-fired water heaters - commercial

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory definition

**Commercial oil-fired water heater**: A water heater that uses oil for fuel, has an input rate of greater than **30.5 kW (105,000 Btu/h)** and that has a **V<sub>r</sub>** of at least **76 L (20 US gallons)**.

### Compliance date

Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2020**

### Compliant product models

[Oil-fired water heaters, commercial](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=WH.Oil-fired.Commercial&page=1) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Testing standard

**10 C.F.R. Appendix A**
