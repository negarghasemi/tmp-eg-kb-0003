# Freezers

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition
**Freezer**: A household freezer that has a capacity of **850 L (30 cubic feet)** or less.

### Compliance Date
Manufactured on or after **September 15, 2014**
_For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations._

### Compliant Product Models
[Freezers](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=Freezers) as listed on NRCans searchable database.

### Labelling Requirements
**EnerGuide label required**

### Testing Standard
**CAN/CSA-C300-15**

### Energy Efficiency Regulations by Province

| :----------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ |
| **8** Upright freezers with manual defrost                 | 0.197 AV + 193.7                            |

_**kWh/year** = kilowatt-hours per year_

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements
- **Name of product**
- **Brand name**
- **Model number**
- **Manufacturer**
- The name of the **certification body or province** whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
- Whether a **mathematical model** as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
- **Annual energy consumption** in kWh
- **Type of freezer**
- **Total refrigerated volume**
- **Adjusted volume**
- **Ice freezing capacity** in kilograms per 24 hours
