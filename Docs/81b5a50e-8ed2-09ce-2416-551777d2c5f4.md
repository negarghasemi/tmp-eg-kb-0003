# Ceiling fans and ceiling fan light kits

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory Definition

*   **Ceiling fan**: A non-portable device suspended from a ceiling for circulating air via the rotation of fan blades.
    *   **Exclusions**: Does not include ceiling fans where the plane of rotation of blades is >45 degrees from horizontal, centrifugal ceiling fans, belt-driven ceiling fans, oscillating ceiling fans, or ceiling fans with a maximum rotational speed of 90 RPM and a maximum airflow of 52.1 cubic metres/minute (1840 cubic feet/minute).
    *   **Types**: Very small diameter, standard, hugger, high-speed small-diameter, or large-diameter.
*   **Ceiling fan light kit**: Equipment designed to provide light from a ceiling fan, either integral to the fan before retail sale or attachable after retail sale.

### Compliance Date

*   **Ceiling fans**: Manufactured on or after **January 21, 2020**.
*   **Ceiling fan light kits**: Manufactured on or after **January 1, 2010**.

*For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations.*

### Compliant Product Models

*   [Ceiling fans and ceiling fan light kits listed on NRCans searchable database](https://spl-lpi.nrcan-rncan.gc.ca/en-US/product/?product=CeilingFans_FanLightKits)

### Testing Standard

*   **Ceiling fans**: 10 C.F.R. Appendix U [Appendix U to Subpart B, Part 430 of Title 10 to the United States *Code of Federal Regulations*, entitled *Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Ceiling Fans*]
*   **Ceiling fan light kits**:
    *   10 C.F.R. 430.23(x)(1) [paragraph 430.23(x)(1) of Subpart B, Part 430 of Title 10 to the United *Code of Federal Regulations*]
    *   CSA C22.2 No. 250.0-08 - Luminaires

*Some provinces also have their own energy efficiency regulations for many of the federally regulated products  [find out which ones!](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/energy-products-regulated-federally-provincially)*

### Energy Efficiency Standard

#### Energy performance standard for ceiling fans

| Product type                          | Minimum efficiency (CFM/W) |
| :------------------------------------ | :------------------------- |
| Very small-diameter (VSD) D  12 in   | 21                         |
| Very small-diameter (VSD) D > 12 in   | 3.16 D 17.04              |
| Standard                              | 0.65 D + 38.03             |
| Hugger                                | 0.29 D + 34.46             |
| High-speed small-diameter (HSSD)      | 4.16 D + 0.02              |
| Large-diameter*                       |                            |

*   **D**: ceiling fan's blade span/diameter, in inches
*   **CFM**: cubic feet per minute
*   **W**: watts
*   *Large-diameter ceiling fans are regulated with no minimum energy performance standard (MEPS). They are subject to testing, verification, and reporting requirements.*

### Energy Efficiency Report Requirements

To download and submit an energy efficiency report, sign in to the [Compliance Regulatory Energy Efficiency Database (CREED)](https://energy-reporting-rapports-energetiques.canada.ca/creed/s/). The report must include:

*   Name of product
*   Brand name
*   Model number
*   Manufacturer
*   Name of the certification body or province whose verification mark will be on the product or its package
*   Whether a mathematical model as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below
*   **For ceiling fans**:
    *   Type
    *   Blade span
    *   Airflow at high speed
    *   Ceiling fan efficiency
    *   Standby power consumption, expressed in watts
*   **For ceiling fan light kits**:
    *   If it has at least one socket, the type of socket and the number of each type of socket
    *   If it has integrated solid-state lighting circuitry, the lighting efficacy, expressed in lumens per watt
    *   If it has a pin-based socket, the ballast type
    *   If it is packaged with lamps, their type, brand name and model number
    *   Total power for the lighting, expressed in watts
