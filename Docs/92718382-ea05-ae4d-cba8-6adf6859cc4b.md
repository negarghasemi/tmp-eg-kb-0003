# Energy Journey for Renters

## Energy Saving Tips and Programs

### Gaming System Energy Consumption
**Is your gaming system costing you?**

*   You'd be surprised to learn how much energy your console uses when it's not in use.
*   Powering down after gameplay can save energy. *Just make sure to save your progress!* 
*   Most gaming systems have a power-saving mode that can help cut down on energy use. [Learn how to enable this feature for your console.](https://www.energystar.gov/products/configuring_todays_game_consoles_use_less_energy_0)

### Conserve Energy in the Kitchen
**Conserve energy in the kitchen!**

You can decrease energy waste everyday by:

*   Reducing your use of lights and appliances.
*   Decluttering your fridge.
*   Cutting excess oven preheating time.

[Try out no-bake recipes](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Advice-and-Tips/No-bake-strawberry-cheesecake-dessert-jars) to keep you energized and help save on your homes energy at the same time.

### Energy Affordability Program
**Looking for low-income help? Check out the Energy Affordability Program.**

Eligible low-income residents can take advantage of free home upgrades, such as:

*   Energy-efficient shower heads
*   Appliances
*   Weatherstripping

[Find out more here](https://www.saveonenergy.ca/For-Your-Home/Energy-Affordability-Program).
