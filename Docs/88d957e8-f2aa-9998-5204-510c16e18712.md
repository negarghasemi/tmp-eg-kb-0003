# Pool Pumps - Infographic

_This infographic highlights the energy efficiency benefits of ENERGY STAR certified pool pumps._

## Make a Savings Splash with a New Pool Pump

**ENERGY STAR certified pool pumps** are designed to **save money, save energy**, and **protect the planet**.

### Your Home's Second Largest Energy Drain

Your pool pump could be the **second largest energy user** in your home, potentially adding about **$580 every year** to your energy bills.

### Significant Savings with ENERGY STAR

*   **ENERGY STAR certified in-ground pool pumps** use up to **65% less energy** than standard pool pumps.
*   This can lead to savings of up to **$245 each and every year**.
*   The investment in an ENERGY STAR pool pump typically sees a **payback in just over two years**.

### Smarter Technology for Pool Pumps

*   ENERGY STAR certified pool pumps are available for both **in-ground and above-ground pools**.
*   They save energy by **adjusting power** based on the task, using more for vacuuming and less for filtration.

### Collective Impact on Energy and Environment

*   If all Canadian pool pumps were ENERGY STAR certified, families could collectively save **$100 million in energy costs every year**.
*   This energy saving would annually prevent greenhouse gas emissions equivalent to those from **more than 60,000 cars**.

### When to Consider a New Pool Pump

It might be time to call a pool services contractor if your current pump shows these symptoms:

*   It seems less powerful than in the past.
*   It makes excessive noise.
*   It occasionally gets hot and shuts off.
*   It won't start or starts slowly.
*   Your pump is **seven to ten years old**.

### Utility Rebates

Many utilities and efficiency organizations offer **rebates** towards the purchase of a new ENERGY STAR certified pool pump. Check local programs for potential savings.

### Learn More

Visit [energystar.gc.ca](http://energystar.gc.ca) for more tips and information on saving energy and money, and protecting the climate.
