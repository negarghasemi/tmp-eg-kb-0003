# Storage tank water heaters

## Energy Efficiency Overview

An **ENERGY STAR certified storage tank water heater** uses **14% less energy**, on average, than a standard model. This leads to **savings on energy costs** and a **reduced carbon footprint**.

### Key Features of ENERGY STAR Certified Models

-   **Improved tank insulation**: Minimizes heat loss.
-   **More efficient heat exchangers**: Transfer more heat from the energy source to the water.
-   **Built-in heat traps**: Reduce heat loss from pipes and tanks.
-   **Cold-water inlet pipes**: Located on the bottoms of tanks to improve heating efficiency.
-   **Gas-fired models** (particularly energy efficient):
    -   **Electronic ignition**: Replaces a continuous burning pilot light.
    -   **Condensing heat exchangers**: For higher overall efficiency.
    -   **Improved control over flue baffles and dampers**: Reduces heat loss through vents.
    -   **Powered venting**.

### Product Information and Comparison

Start your research using the [ENERGY STAR Product Finder tool](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-water-heaters/results?SetLanguage=English&NRCAN=on) to find and compare certified water heaters. 

*Note: Only gas-fired storage tank water heaters can qualify for ENERGY STAR. You will be redirected to the US ENERGY STAR website. Make sure to click Gas Storage under Type when searching.*

### Helpful Energy Saving Tips

-   **Insulate pipes**: Insulating the hot and cold water pipes within 2 to 3 meters of the water heater is the most cost-effective improvement for existing or new installations.
-   **Reduce consumption**: Consider how you and your family use hot water and take steps to reduce consumption.
-   **Further guidance**: Learn more in the booklet [*Water Heater Guide*](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/sites/nrcan/files/oee/files/pdf/equipment/WaterHeaterGuide_e.pdf) (PDF - 2.8 MB).

### Energy Labels Explained

Storage tank water heaters in Canada can have two labels:

-   **The ENERGY STAR symbol**: Indicates the model is highly energy efficient.
-   **The voluntary EnerGuide label**: Shows the models energy consumption and how it compares to similar models. [Learn more about EnerGuide labels](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/product-energy-ratings/energuide/energuide-label).

### The ENERGY STAR Promise

All ENERGY STAR certified products are tested to meet **strict efficiency specifications** and are certified by an **independent third party**. They perform the same as or better than standard products without compromising performance in any way.

### Regulations

Water heaters are subject to **Canada's *Energy Efficiency Regulations***, which set a minimum performance standard for their energy efficiency. Find details in the [Guide to the Regulations](http://www.nrcan.gc.ca/energy/regulations-codes-standards/6861).
