# Home Renovation Savings Program Overview

Enbridge Gas and Save on Energy, with support from the Ontario Government, have partnered to launch the new **Home Renovation Savings program** to help Ontarians improve home energy efficiency and comfort. Homeowners now have more choice, with flexible options to choose single or bundled upgrades for greater savings.

## Energy Efficiency Upgrades and Rebates

### Upgrades Requiring a Home Energy Assessment (Do two or more upgrades)
*Rebates that require a home energy assessment. To get started, refer to the program's guidelines for assessment details.*

| Upgrade Category                     | Max Rebate      |
|--------------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Insulation** (attic, wall, foundation, exposed floor) | **Up to $7,700**|
| **Windows and doors**                | **$100 per rough opening** |
| **Heat pump water heaters**          | **$500**        |
| **Air sealing**                      | **Up to $250**  |

### Upgrades Not Requiring a Home Energy Assessment (Do one or more upgrades)
*Rebates that do not require a home energy assessment. To get started, refer to the program's guidelines for these upgrades.*

| Upgrade Category                     | Max Rebate      |
|--------------------------------------|-----------------|
| **Heat pumps**                       | **Up to $12,000** |
| **Solar panels and battery storage** | **Up to $10,000** |
| **Attic insulation**                 | **$1,250** (*previously $1,000*) |
| **Smart thermostats**                | **$100** (*previously $75*) |
| **Appliances**                       | **Up to $200**  |
