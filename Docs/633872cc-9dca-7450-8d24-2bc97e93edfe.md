# Torchieres

## Energy Efficiency Regulations

### Regulatory definition

**Torchiere:** *A portable luminaire that has a reflector bowl or similar-shaped reflector that directs light in a predominantly upward direction for the purpose of providing indirect lighting and that may be equipped with one or more additional sockets intended for other lighting functions.*

### Compliance date

*   **Manufactured on or after:** January 1, 2010
*   *For models manufactured prior, refer to the Regulations*

### Testing standard

*   **CSA C22.2 No. 250.4-14**

### Energy efficiency standard

*Energy performance standard for torchieres*

| Product type                                       | Total power |
| :------------------------------------------------- | :---------- |
| **Torchieres that have no additional sockets**     |  75 W      |
| **Torchieres that have one or more additional sockets** |  100 W     |

*W = watts*

### Energy efficiency report requirements

*   **Name of product**
*   **Brand name**
*   **Model number**
*   **Manufacturer**
*   **Name of the certification body or province** *whose verification mark will be on the product or its package*
*   **Indication** *of whether a mathematical model as defined in the Regulations was used to generate any of the information provided below*
*   **Information** *that indicates whether the product has lighting functions additional to those of the bowl*
*   **Total power, expressed in watts,** *for all lighting functions*
