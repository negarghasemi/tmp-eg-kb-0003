# Find the facts about electric heat pumps

Did you know **99% of home emissions in Canada are from heating systems**? Electric heat pumps offer many benefits and are energy efficient. They are a clean, proven, and reliable technology. They offer a great way for homeowners and landlords to save energy. Find the facts about electric heat pumps below.

## Myths and facts on electric heat pumps

### Myth 1: Heat pumps cant handle our Canadian winters.

**Fact:** Cold climate heat pumps work in temperatures as low as -30C. Backup heating systems also run cost effectively and will keep your home warm on the coldest days.

*Learn more about how heat pumps warm your home.*

### Myth 2: Heat pumps are only for heating.

**Fact:** Despite its name, a heat pump can both heat and cool your home year-round.

*Learn more about heating and cooling with a heat pump.*

### Myth 3: Having a heat pump will increase my energy bill.

**Fact:** Heat pumps use less energy than traditional equipment to heat and cool your home. They are both efficient and climate friendly and can save you money. Costs vary by household and region. So, to understand the potential impacts of heating equipment changes on individual homes, we always recommend an EnerGuide home evaluation from a registered energy advisor.

### Myth 4: Heat pumps are too loud.

**Fact:** Modern heat pumps are often quieter than traditional heating and AC systems.

*Learn more about heat pump basics.*
