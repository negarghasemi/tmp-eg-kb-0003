# Energy-using products regulated federally and provincially*

*The table below does not include energy-using products that are only regulated at the provincial level.

The federal [Energy Efficiency Regulations, 2016](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2016-311/index.html) were last amended through [Amendment 18](https://natural-resources.canada.ca/energy-efficiency/energy-efficiency-regulations/amendment-18). All new requirements will come into force on October 9, 2025, with product compliance dates noted in the Regulations (see [amendments not yet in force](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2016-311/index.html#:~:text=Amendments%20Not%20In%20Force)).

British Columbia, Manitoba, Ontario, Quebec, New Brunswick and Nova Scotia have their own energy efficiency regulations for many of the federally regulated energy-using products.
