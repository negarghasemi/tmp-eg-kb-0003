# Grants for Canadian Homeowners in North and Off-Grid Communities

**Note:** The Canada Greener Homes Grant program closed on **December 31, 2025**. Requests submitted by this deadline will continue to be processed.

Canadians living in northern and off-grid communities faced specific challenges with home retrofits, leading to increased grants (an additional 30%) for these regions. Homeowners in northern and off-grid communities were eligible for replacements of existing fossil-fuel burning equipment. Northern communities also had additional insulation measures (attic, ceiling, exposed floor insulation).

## Canada Greener Homes Initiative Offerings:

*   **Grants:** From **$162.50 to $5,000** for eligible and recommended home retrofits.
*   **EnerGuide Evaluations:** Up to **$600** towards pre- and post-retrofit EnerGuide evaluations if at least one eligible retrofit was completed.
*   **Interest-free Loans:** Up to **$40,000** with a 10-year repayment term for major home retrofits.

**Maximum Grant Amount:** A household could receive up to **$5,600** in total (up to $5,000 for retrofits and up to $600 for evaluations).

*Note: If you live in Ontario, Quebec, or Nova Scotia, specific provincial information may apply.*

### Ineligible Retrofits

*   Retrofits completed **before** a pre-retrofit evaluation.
*   Retrofits completed **before December 1, 2020**.
*   Products installed in new home additions built between pre- and post-retrofit EnerGuide evaluations (e.g., new insulation in additions, new windows/doors in additions, components requiring 100% upgrades like crawl space wall/header insulation, exposed floor insulation).
*   Leased or rented equipment.
*   Air conditioners.
*   Tankless or storage water heaters (other than heat pump water heaters).
*   Skylights.
*   Roofs.
*   Windows and doors in a garage.
